On this occasion was honored by Dr. Lincoln Chen, President of the China Medical Board, USA attended the meeting including to be a guest speaker. The meeting was supported by The China Medical Board, USA. SEANERN is a network of Nursing School Deans and senior faculty in Southeast and East Asia who meet annually to discuss curriculum development and quality assurance, to promote research and foster partnerships in teaching and experiential student placements.
