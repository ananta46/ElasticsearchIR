The delegation was honored by the presence of Assistant Professor Dr. Pinpinut Wanichsaithong, Assistant Dean for Strategy and Organizational Development, along with the Faculty Secretary and team leaders from the Faculty of Dentistry, who warmly welcomed the delegation. The ICDI participants included the College’s Secretary, Head of General Administration, and over 10 support staff members.
