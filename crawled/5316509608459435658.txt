Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News Students from the International College of Digital Innovation Chiang Mai University received a Best Oral Presentation Award at the international conference “The 3rd Trilateral Symposium on Sustainability – Strategies for Climate Action and Mitigation of Climate Change Impacts.” 2 September 2024 International College of Digital Innovation Congratulations to Mr. Chanchai Chan, a third-year undergraduate student  in Digital Innovation at the International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, for receiving the Best Oral Presentation Award for his work on “Video Games and Sustainability: Minecraft as a Platform for Education” (Using Minecraft as a Low Code Engagement Tool for the Youth in Teaching SDGs). This award was presented during “The 3rd Trilateral Symposium on Sustainability – Strategies for Climate Action and Mitigation of Climate Change Impacts,” held from August 27th to 29th, 2024, at the Kantary Hills Hotel in Chiang Mai. The symposium was organized by Chiang Mai University in collaboration with its international partners, Kagawa University and National Chiayi University, to promote academic cooperation between the three universities, fostering a network of international research collaborations. The event also provided an opportunity for students from the partner universities to present their work in both Oral Sessions and Poster Sessions.
