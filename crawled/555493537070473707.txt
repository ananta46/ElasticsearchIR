Prof. Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, the Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, assigned the tourism team as the leader of the U2T project in Hai Ya Sub-District to welcome Dr. Danuch Tanterdtid, Secretary of the Minister of Higher Education, Science, Research, and Innovation, Mr. Methee Limniyakul, Director of Network Coordination Group, Asst. The event was appreciated by Phrakru Phithaksutthikhun, the abbot of Wat Sri Suphan, who gave an outlook on the success of the collaboration with the university on the project, both in terms of developing new products such as auspicious bracelets for the businesses of Wat Sri Suphan community, and introducing digital technology to support tourism in Hai Ya community in all aspects. This is considered successful in achieving U2T's goals for BCG in terms of product development, service, and collaborative learning.
