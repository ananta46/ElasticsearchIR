The research was presented at the 35th Annual Meeting of the Thai Society for Biotechnology and the International Conference (TSB 2023) ‘Sustainable Development through Bio-Circular Green (BCG) Economy Model’ held between November 26 – 29, 2023, at The Greenery Resort in Khao Yai, Nakhon Ratchasima.
