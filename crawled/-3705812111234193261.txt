Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News Master Degree Students Majoring in Social Studies visit Sukhothaithammathiraj University, Suankularb College and The Hong Kong Polytechnic Technology University 3 May 2019 Faculty of Education On March 26 – 29, 2019, faculty members from the Master Degree Program of Educational Management led by Associate Professor Dr. Charin Mangkhang, Head Department of Curriculum, Teaching and Learning and Assistant Professor Dr. Chettapoom Wannapaisan, Head of Social Studies and accompanied by faculty members, led 22 students on a study visit to Sukhothaithammathiraj University, Suankularb College and to the Department of Applied Social Sciences, Faculty of Health and Social Sciences, Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hong Kong. This study visit was an opportunity to exchange knowledge and the visiting CMU Master Degree students together with the host institutions had an opportunity to learn more together about education innovation.
