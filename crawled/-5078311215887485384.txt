Assistant Professor Dr. Charnnarong Srisuwan, the Dean, had given an opening speech on March 10, 2020 at Faculty of Architecture Alumni Association Office. This training course for youth on drawing, painting and photographing was organized until March 14, 2020 having professors from the Faculty of Architecture and special speakers to give knowledge to students in both theoretical and practical aspect. The faculty had prepared hand sanitizing gel for hygienic purpose in the area, distributed hygienic masks to all students and took everyone’s temperature every day during the training program. Moreover, the training areas, tools and furniture are all sanitized against COVID-19 following Protective Measures Against the Coronavirus (COVID – 19).
