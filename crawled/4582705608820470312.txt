Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News ICDI and NXPO discuss collaborating to develop innovation-based entrepreneurs. 8 February 2023 International College of Digital Innovation On February 7, 2023, Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation (ICDI), Chiang Mai University, and Dr. Piang-or Loahavilai, ICDI expert in cross-border e-commerce, joined the executives and staff of the ICDI in welcoming Dr. Kitipong Promwong, Director of the Office of the National Higher Education Science Research and Innovation Policy Council (NXPO), Ms. Benjamas Mahawongkajit, Director of the Budget Division, and Ms. Tippawan Vechakaran, Director of the Secretariat Office of the Science Park Promotion Committee. The dean has welcomed all the visitors on the occasion of leading the executives and working groups from the NXPO, Office of the Ministry of Higher Education, Science, Research, and Innovation, and the Bureau of the Budget to participate in the meeting to follow up on the progress of cooperation projects to develop innovation-based entrepreneurs at ICDI as well as to visit Yunda Express Chiangmai, a Thai-Chinese freight forwarding company that sees logistics as the key to development as it is crucial to encourage the future growth of e-commerce among Thai people.
