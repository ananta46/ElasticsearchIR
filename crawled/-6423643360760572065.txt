Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News Chiang Mai University Veterinary Medicine Conference 2021 15 December 2021 Faculty of Veterinary Medicine 8 December 2021: Faculty of Veterinary Medicine, Chiang Mai University held “Chiang Mai University Veterinary Medicine Conference 2021 (CVMC)” theme “New Normal in Veterinary Medicine” and it was composed of a virtual online of seminars on 7th December 2021 and and onsite conference on 8th – 9th December 2021, and delivered the opening remarks by President of Chiang Mai University Clinical Professor Niwes Nantachit (MD) and chairman of the opening ceremony and Dean Khwanchai Kreausukon gave a remark address. The conference featured a variety of seminars such as keynote speeches on “How to deal with “New Normal” in Veterinary Medicine” by Associate Professor Dr. Theera Rakkhwamsuk, President of Veterinary Council. It provided a panel discussion on animal health situation update in the Northern region in 2021 including oral and poster presentations at Faculty of Veterinary Medicine, Chiang Mai University, Room E115.
