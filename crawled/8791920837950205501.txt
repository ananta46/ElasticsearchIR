The five members are: 1.\tPichparnee Boonpradab, from the Environmental Science programme, Faculty of Science (Student ID: 620515005), 2.\tTanadol Deachprapakorn, from the Computer Engineering programme, Faculty of Engineering (Student ID: 630610734), 3.\tPanupong Kiaengkham, from the Data Science programme, Faculty of Science (Student ID: 620510695), 4.\tPongsakorn Rattanapan, from the Computer Engineering programme, Faculty of Engineering (Student ID: 630610749), and 5.\tWan Wiriya, from the Department of Chemistry, Faculty of Science, and the Environmental Science Research Centre (ESRC).
