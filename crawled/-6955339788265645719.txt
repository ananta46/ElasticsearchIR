The students selected to participate are: 1) Ms. Thitirat Charoensawan 2) Ms. Kammaporn Kangwalwattanasiri 3) Mr. Athinan Tangchiamsri 4) Mr. Pantuch Sungkaew This selection is part of the Huawei Seeds for the Future program, a global corporate social responsibility initiative launched by Huawei in Thailand in 2008. The program aims to bring together exceptional students from top universities worldwide to engage in digital training camps. In Thailand, the Seeds Booth Camp was conducted for 16 students at the G-Tower in Bangkok and in Si Racha district, Chonburi Province, from August 30 to September 1, 2024. The camp provided training on cutting-edge topics such as 5G, clean energy, digital transformation, and cloud computing. Additionally, participants worked on creating TECH4Good activity plans focused on \"Utilizing Technology to Address Social Issues,\" with support and guidance from experts at DEPA and Huawei.
