The conference promoted research and exchange of knowledge in the field of Business Administration between both national and international participants. CMU’s Faculty of Business Administration Dean Associate Professor Dr. Siriwut Buranapira, Chairperson of Thailand Regional Business School Network provided the welcome remark and co-presided over the opening ceremony together with Associate Professor Dr. Pensri Charoenwanich, Dean, Faculty of Business and Accounting, Khon Kaen University, Assistant Professor Dr. Punnee Pimapunsri, Dean, Faculty of Management and Tourism, Burapa University and Assistant Professor Theerawat Hangsapreuk, Dean, Faculty of Scientific Management, Prince of Songkhla University. During the conference a keynote speech was presented by Dr. Edward Rubesch, Program Director, Innovation Driven Entrepreneurship Center, University of Thai Chamber of Commerce, Bangkok, on “Business Sustainability in Digitalization Era”.  The conference also had over 200 participants and 74 research articles were presented of which 8 were awarded prizes for their outstanding results.
