The purpose of this event is to engage in acts of merit, foster the principles of Buddhism, and enhance the overall success of the institution. Moreover, it serves as a source of inspiration for the personnel of ICDI. The ceremony was presided over by Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, the Dean of ICDI, and was  attended by administrators, faculty, and staff.
