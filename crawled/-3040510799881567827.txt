Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News Discussion meeting on academic collaboration and cooperative education between CMU Economics and Engineering faculties and Iron and Steel Institute of Thailand, Thailand Automotive Institute and Plastics Institute of Thailand 27 January 2024 Corporate Communication and Alumni Relations Center (CCARC) On January 27, 2024, at ECB 2602, CMU Faculty of Economics, Professor Dr. Pongruk Sribanditmongkol, CMU President, welcomed Mr. Anusorn Nuangpolmak, President of the Iron and Steel Institute of Thailand (ISIT) and Chairman of the Thailand Automotive Institute (TAI), Mr. Wirote Rotewatanachai, Director of ISIT, Dr. Kriengsak Wongpromrat, Director of TAI, and Dr. Julathep Kajornchaiyakul, Director of the Plastics Institute of Thailand (PITH), to attend the discussion meeting on academic collaboration and cooperative education between CMU Faculties of Economics and Engineering and the three institutes. The meeting aimed to establish guidelines in curriculum design to meet the future needs and development of CMU. Also in attendance were Associate Professor Anawat Wisetborisut, M.D., Director of the Office of Educational Quality Development, Assistant Professor Dr. Pairat Kanjanakaroon, Dean of the Faculty of Economics, and Associate Professor Dr. Thongchai Fongsamootr, Dean of the Faculty of Engineering, along with involved administrators and staff.
