The visit aimed to discuss academic cooperation and explore potential collaborations between the two institutions. Led by Asst. Prof. Dr. Thanee Kaewthummanukul, Dean of the Faculty of Nursing, administrators from NurseCMU, including Asst. Prof. Dr. Jutarat Mesukko, Associate Dean responsible for Research, Innovation, and Global Mission Integration, and Dr. Preeyakamon Krikitrat, Assistant to the Dean responsible for Global Mission Integration, received the delegation from Wenzhou Medical University. Dr. Preeyakamon led the delegation on a tour of the faculty's nursing learning unit, library, and historical museum. This visit provided an excellent opportunity for both institutions to exchange ideas and explore potential collaborations in the nursing field. The Faculty of Nursing at Chiang Mai University is dedicated to fostering international partnerships and collaborations to enhance the quality of education and research in nursing. The Wenzhou Medical University delegation's visit is essential to achieving this goal. \"We are delighted to host our colleagues from Wenzhou Medical University and look forward to exploring opportunities for collaboration to promote academic excellence,\" said Asst. Prof. Dr. Thanee Kaewthummanukul. Participants from Wenzhou Medical University. 1. Prof. Cheng Jinguo, Deputy Party Secretary and Executive Chairperson of the WMU National Center for International TCM Collaboration 2. Prof. Wang Shize, Dean, College of Continuing Education 3.
