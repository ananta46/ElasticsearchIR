This visit was a part of The Future-Link :Chiangmai  University and The University of Melbourne “Future cities -Disaster resilience and sustainable energy collaborations” project under the Person to Organization Program (P2O)  of Chiang Mai University, aiming for collaboration towards future cities, disaster resilience, and sustainable energy. They were warmly welcomed by Professor Ron Wakefield, Dean of the School of Property, Construction, and Project Management at RMIT, and Professor Katherine Johnson, Dean of the School of Global, Urban, and Social Studies, together with RMIT faculty members. The visit focused on fostering academic collaboration, research, and exchange of personnel and students for mutual future development, held on July 23, 2024, in Melbourne, Australia.
