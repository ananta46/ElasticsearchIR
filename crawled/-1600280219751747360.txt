The emerging institute was entitled “Energy Research and Development Institute, Chiang Mai University (ERDI-CMU). The institute is aimed to be a center of excellence in terms of research and development of biogas and all types of energy. Later, H.R.H. Princess Maha Chakri Sirindhorn bestowed the name “Energy Research and Development Institute-Nakornping, Chiang Mai University” on the institute. The main objectives of the institute include energy research, knowledge development, application of knowledge with energy and environmental crises, and biogas development and usage to attain the highest advantages. It is anticipated that the institute will successfully become the center of professionalism and knowledge in terms of energy of the country and the ASEAN region. ERDI-CMU was gracefully named by H.R.H. Princess Maha Chakri Sirindhorn as “Energy Research and Development Institute-Nakornping, Chiang Mai University” Vision “To Become Leading Institute on Renewable Energy in the ASEAN Region” Mission \u0010 Conduct research and create innovation on renewable energy responding to the ASEAN requirement \u0010 Provide academic services on energy and environment to be up to the international standard \u0010 Develop efficiency of the overall management system Operations The operations of Energy Research and Development Institute-Nakornping, Chiang Mai University mainly focused on renewable energy, energy conservation, energy research and development as well as academic services. 1. Renewable Energy ERDI offers research and development services on renewable energy especially on biogas, biomass and biodiesel. Such services provided to both Government and Private sectors. 2. Energy Conservation ERDI promotes energy conservation in buildings and industries such as energy conservation consultation, energy auditing, and effects from energy consumption. 3. Research and Development Projects ERDI linked its research projects to serve all level of educational institute. The qualified and useful academic researches have been distributed to external communities. As well, ERDI attempts to expand its research network in national and international levels. 4. Academic Services ERDI provided academic services on energy conservation, renewable energy and Clean Development Mechanism (CDM) by linking up its research projects to the academic services in industrial and economical sections both in local and international levels.
