Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News The delegation from International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University visited the headquarters of Long-Spring Education Group and the Yunnan Province Preparatory College for Overseas Study in Kunming, Yunnan Province, People's Republic of China. 28 December 2023 International College of Digital Innovation On December 26, 2023, from 4:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m., and on December 27, 2023, from 9:00 a.m. to 10:00 a.m. (local time in the People's Republic of China), the delegation from International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, led by Assistant Professor Dr. Benjamas Suksatit, Deputy Dean, visited the headquarters of Long-Spring Education Group and the Yunnan Province Preparatory College for Overseas Study in Kunming, Yunnan Province, People's Republic of China. The visit aimed to introduce and publicize admissions to international programs, specifically the Bachelor's degree programs at Chiang Mai University, consisting of 17 courses available through the CMU-IPAS channel. During the visit, the delegation was warmly welcomed by Mr. Wang Lei, Executive Dean of Yunnan Province Preparatory College for Overseas Study. The team took the opportunity to present and promote the Bachelor's degree programs offered by Chiang Mai University. A total of 54 high school students, who expressed interest in pursuing studies at Chiang Mai University, were identified during the sessions. These students are now being considered as potential candidates for enrollment in the international programs at Chiang Mai University, with the aim of preparing them for the next steps in their academic journey.
