Prof. Dr. Suthaphat Kamthai from the Faculty of Agro-industry, Chiang Mai University, received the Rice Innovation Award 2021 in the industrial category from Princess Maha Chakri Sirindhorn for the work titled, ‘Rice Supply Chain Biodegradable Packaging Innovation’. CMU Science and Technology Park (CMU STeP) assisted in the project’s business planning and marketing.
