Prof. Dr. Apichoke Lekagul, Vice Dean, along with executives and heads of department of the Faculty of Architecture, Chiang Mai University, were welcomed by Dr. Ying-Hung Li, Associate Dean. They also visited the university's learning center, which was designed as a learning space for students, and the ROSOCoop Center, a joint research project that uses advanced robotics technology and computational design to create new innovations for the construction industry on March 1, 2023. The activity is conducted under the fieldtrip project to enhance the management efficiency of the executives of the Faculty of Architecture, Chiang Mai University. The aim is to apply successful case studies and ideas from international institutions to develop policies, strategies, and plans to elevate the organization to an international level. The activity also aims to enhance the working experience and networking with academic partners from other countries.
