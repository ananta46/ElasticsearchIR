The purpose of this event was to provide students with knowledge and a deeper understanding of Japan and Japanese culture. The event was graciously supported by Vice Consul Sugiki Takahiro and Ms. Namita Waraniran, an information and culture officer, along with Ms. Waraporn Natesin, an educational advisor for studying in Japan, from the Consulate-General of Japan in Chiang Mai, who served as guest speakers.
