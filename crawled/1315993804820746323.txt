The 13th academic seminar, held on December 8th, 2024, focused on Medication Prescription of BSN-Prepared/Advanced Practice Nurses: Current Status and Challenges. The seminar was conducted through the Zoom Meeting with Universidad Nacional de Colombia serving as the host. During the seminar, Assistant Professor Dr. Rangsiya Narin, who represented the Faculty of Nursing's Public Health Nursing, shared valuable insights on the subject matter.
