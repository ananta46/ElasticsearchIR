Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News The International College of Digital Innovation, CMU underwent an organizational quality assessment in accordance with CMU-EdPEx guidelines for the academic year 2021 30 November 2022 International College of Digital Innovation On November 30, 2022, the International College of Digital Innovation underwent an organizational quality assessment in accordance with CMU-EdPEx guidelines for the academic year 2021 in order to: (1) receive an organizational quality assessment with EdPEx criteria and confirm the performance of the division, (2) to identify the division's self-awareness, team potential, and division potential, including the department's situation, and (3) to receive suggestions and guidelines to drive the development of the organization to excellence. The activity was organized in an on-site format at the ICB1313 meeting room with honors from the following committees: (1) Asst. Prof. Dr. Sitcha Siriaree, M.D., Faculty of Medicine, as the Chairman,(2) Asst. Prof. Dr. Weenun Bundit, Assistant to the President, as the committee member, (3) Assoc. Prof. Dr. Arthit Phuangmali, Faculty of Associated Medical Sciences, as the committee member, and (4) Assoc. Prof. Dr. Pichaya Poolarp, Faculty of Agro-Industry, as the committee member. The session is divided into 3 phases: (1) Performance presentation by the Senior Leader team of the College, led by Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation. (2) Interviews with the senior leader management team to share information on the organization's commitment to innovation (3) The management team and staff of Digital Innovation International College listen to the assessment results.
