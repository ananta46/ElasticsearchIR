Demonstration School 15 September 2022 International College of Digital Innovation The International College of Digital Innovation, CMU, has an open house event to welcome M5 students from Chiang Mai University Demonstration School The Undergraduate Education Services Unit of the International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University has organized a course guidance activity for Mattayom 5 students from the Demonstration School of Chiang Mai University in order to introduce digital innovation courses and future admission criteria, which will help students to get proper information about the right course for their purposes and to provide information for future study at the university level on September 14, 2022, at the International College of Digital Innovation, CMU, led by Asst. Prof. Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of ICDI, gave the welcome speech for the event. A total of 30 students participated in the activity, along with one guidance teacher. The event was honored by the secretary of the digital innovation undergraduate course, Dr. Chakkrapong Kueansaen, who gave information on the course that focuses on entrepreneurship, the ability to create startup projects, and admission information. In addition, Ajarn Aniwat Phaphuangwittayakul has also introduced Metaverse, SNIPER, and other activities of the college, and Dr. Naret Suyaroj, Secretary of Digital Innovation and Financial Technology Program for graduate level, and the team is the one who recommends equipment and programs to prepare to enter the world of Metaverse fully based on virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) through a demonstration using the Microsoft HoloLens supervised tool in the FABLAB laboratory.
