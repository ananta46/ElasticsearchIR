This year, CMU’s best performing subject is Nursing, where it places at No 101 – 150 globally, sharing the national top spot.  CMU has also earned its first entry in Economics & Econometrics at No 451- 500, as well as reappearances by Engineering – Electrical & Electronic at No 451- 500 and Physics & Astronomy at 551 – 600, respectively. For Broad Subjects, CMU is ranked at No 313 in the world in Life Sciences & Medicine, and 451- 500 in Natural Sciences, marking the first ever appearance in this category, while successfully making a comeback in Arts & Humanities and Engineering & Technology at No 451 – 500 and 501 -550, respectively. Thai universities’ entries in Broad Subjects Thai universities’ entries in Narrow Subjects The five metrics used in the QS World University Rankings by Subject are 1) Academic reputation, 2) Employer reputation, 3) Citations per paper, 4) H-index and 5) International Research Network. The weighting of each metric varies by discipline.
