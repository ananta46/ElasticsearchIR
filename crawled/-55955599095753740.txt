Kitakyushu in Japan 14 February 2023 Faculty of Architecture Professor Dr. Rawiwan Oranratmanee, Dean, together with other executives of Faculty of Architecture visited Faculty of Environmental Engineering, The University of Kitakyushu in Japan on Tuesday, 14 February 2023, and was welcomed by Prof. Bart Dewancker, Head of Department of Architecture and the executive team who gave them a tour around the campus and visited to Prof. Hiroatsu Fukuda's Meldia Research Institute for Advanced Wood. To end the day, the two faculties signed the Memorandum of Understanding between Faculty of Architecture, Chiang Mai University and Faculty of Environmental Engineering, The University of Kitakyushu to expand the academic and research collaboration which was a part of the One Faculty One MoU Project Under the Quick Win Project, Plan 13 Policy, Chiang Mai University. The aim of this collaboration is to encourage, promote, and support academic collaborations with existing or new partners, creating a mutually beneficial exchange of knowledge, research, and innovation for students and staff.
