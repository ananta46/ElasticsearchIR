Body: A Mission to Conquer Calories, under the project to promote the organization of happiness (Happy Workplace) for the fiscal year 2023. 30 March 2023 International College of Digital Innovation Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation, presided over the award ceremony for the winners of the \"ICDI Mini Walk & Fun Run (Season 2)\" activity, exercising to create happiness, Happy & Healthy Body: A Mission to Conquer Calories, under the project to promote the organization of happiness (Happy Workplace) for the fiscal year 2023. The activities were conducted by ICDI academic and supporting staff. A total of 24 people participated in the activity, divided into 8 teams, over a 36-day cumulative period between February 8, 2023, and March 15, 2023, with a ceremony to announce the results and awards. On Tuesday, March 28, 2023, at 4:30 p.m. in the activity area on the 1st floor of the International College of Digital Innovation building. 1st prize: 3,000 baht with medals Team: Blood type A, B, O Team members: 1.
