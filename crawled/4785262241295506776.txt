Prof. Dr. Suparat Wangsrikhun, Dean of the Faculty of Nursing, together with the faculty's executive team, extended a warm welcome to Professor Dr. Sadakazu Ejiri, Vice President, and Ms. Izumi Den, Manager of International Exchange from Asahi University, Japan. The visit was on the occasion of their participation in the Presidential Forum: Navigating the Future of Higher Education, held from 22–24 January 2025 at Chiang Mai University. . During this visit, delegates from Asahi University and the faculty's executive team participated in a One-On-One Meeting to discuss academic collaboration with Prof. of Practice Dr. Charin Techapun, Vice President of Chiang Mai University. Additionally, the delegation visited the Faculty of Nursing to explore opportunities for collaboration in research, exchange programs, and fostering academic partnerships between the institutions on Thursday, 23 January 2025.
