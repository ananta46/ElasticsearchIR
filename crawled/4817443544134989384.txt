He was recognized as an individual who has applied his knowledge and expertise in practice, promoting and supporting educational development. His outstanding practices have led to the creation of valuable contributions to education. He has dedicated himself selflessly to the benefit of society, adhering to moral principles, ethics, and exemplary conduct. Additionally, he has demonstrated his gratitude to the nation through various activities. This recognition was in accordance with the decision of the Thai Honor Award Committee. The award ceremony was held at Chaiyaphruek 2 Room, Air Force Convention Hall, Bangkok.
