Prof. Dr. Chaiwuth Tangsomchai, a lecturer in Finance, Chiang Mai University Business School, led the International Accounting Program students on an insightful visit to Chiang Mai Fresh Milk Company Limited. Dr.  Bulakul Tipnate, the Chairman of the Board of Directors and Managing Director of the company, extended generous support for this educational outing. The visit aimed to enrich student learning experience and understanding of business operations, management, and how the company adapt during the COVID-19 pandemic. During the experience sharing of the company's management team, including Ms. Thamol Tipnate and Mr. Pasuth Yossakda, students received a warm welcome, gained valuable insights, and had their questions addressed. They also explored the company's facilities to see real-world business practices in action.
