The program featured musical performances by international students, offering a unique showcase of cultural richness. Additionally, participants had the chance to express gratitude for positive experiences and come together for a shared dinner, fostering a spirit of unity and appreciation. The event took place on Thursday, November 23, 2023, at the International College of Digital Innovation, and saw the enthusiastic participation of 79 international students, faculty, and staff members.
