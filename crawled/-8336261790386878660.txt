Led by Lecturers Dr. Tanarat Rattanadamrongaksorn, Dr. Naret Suyaroj, Dr. Chakkrapong Kuensaen, Dr. Saronsad Sokantika, Dr. Suttida Suwannayod, Mr. Thanwa Benjawan, and Mr. Pirapong Wongsaensee, the workshops focused on \"Creating Innovators for Innovation\" and were attended by 393 students. The workshops were held at the McGilvary Conference Room, Sophia Bradley McGilvary Building, and aimed to provide academic and teaching support to the school in the academic year 2023. The faculty members from ICDI shared their expertise and knowledge in creating innovators through interactive sessions and practical activities. This initiative demonstrates the commitment of ICDI to supporting local educational institutions and promoting innovation in education. The workshops provided an excellent opportunity for teachers and educational personnel to gain valuable insights and strategies for cultivating an innovative learning environment. Overall, the collaboration between ICDI and Dara Academy School is a significant step towards achieving concrete results in creating a culture of innovation in education.
