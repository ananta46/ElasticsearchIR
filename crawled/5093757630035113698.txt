The project was awarded the **Silver Prize** in the “Young Rising Stars of Science Award 2024” competition organized by the Science Society of Thailand Under the Patronage of His Majesty the King. Their research was supervised by Assistant Professor Dr. Suphat Phongthai and Dr. Pipat Tangjaidee.
