The library has consistently prioritized environmental sustainability by developing an eco-friendly organization. This dedication has been demonstrated through numerous achievements: 2020: Recognized with the Outstanding Green Library Award after meeting the Green Library Assessment criteria. 2021: Earned Excellent Green Office Certification (Gold Level) for outstanding environmental practices. 2023: Successfully renewed its certification under the Green Library Assessment. 2024: Achieved the ISO 14001:2015 Certification, marking a significant milestone in the library’s journey toward sustainability. Commitment to Environmental Sustainability Chiang Mai University Library is devoted to fostering an environmentally conscious library that raises awareness and promotes energy and environmental conservation among its administrators, staff, and stakeholders. The initiatives include: Encouraging the efficient use of resources and energy. Advocating for reuse and recycling practices. Reducing environmental pollution. Creating a conducive environment for learning, working, and promoting well-being within the organization and the surrounding community. These efforts aim to support sustainable development both internally and externally, serving as a model for eco-friendly practices in higher education institutions. This certification is a source of immense pride for the Chiang Mai University Library, reflecting its unwavering commitment to environmental sustainability and its role as a leader in green initiatives.
