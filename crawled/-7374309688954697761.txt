Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News CMU Library participated in the signing of the Green Library Network Memorandum of Understanding during the 2024 Annual General Meeting of the Green Library Club and the Green Library Network Executive Meeting, aimed at enhancing collaboration and advancing environmental sustainability in library services. 28 November 2024 Chiang Mai University Library Mr. Pratch Sanguansak, Director of Chiang Mai Library, participated in the signing ceremony of the Green Library Network Memorandum of Understanding during the 2024 Annual General Meeting of the Green Library Club and the Executive Meeting of the Green Library Network. This event, organized by the Green Library Club in collaboration with the Library Association of Thailand, aims to foster the development and provision of environmental information services while advancing efforts toward creating carbon-neutral libraries with net-zero greenhouse gas emissions. A total of 49 organizations, encompassing 54 libraries, signed the memorandum of understanding. The ceremony was held at the Kampol Adulvith Conference Room, 50th Anniversary Information Building, Kasetsart University, Bangkhen, Bangkok, on November 21, 2024.
