Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News CMU Library Bureau organized the \"Green Library to the Community\" activity as part of the 5th \"City-Building Tales Festival,\" a reading and learning event for children in Chiang Mai. 12 December 2024 Chiang Mai University Library Mrs. Orapan Kankonsue, Deputy Director of the University Library, together with the Library's executive team, facilitated the \"Waste Sorting Champion\" initiative at the 5th Chiang Mai Storytelling Festival—an esteemed event promoting reading and learning for children in Chiang Mai under the theme \"Healthy Kids, Healthy City: Joyful Children, Serene City.\" This festival was organized by the Chiang Mai Reading Network in collaboration with over 22 other reading organizations, with the objective of nurturing intellectual growth, fostering creativity, and cultivating long-term environmental stewardship among the youth. The aforementioned activity forms part of the \"Green Library to Schools and Communities\" program, spearheaded by the Library's Environmental Management Task Force in adherence to internationally recognized environmental standards. The event was held at the Doi Suthep Nature Center, Chiang Mai University, on December 7, 2024.
