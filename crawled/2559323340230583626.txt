The event focuses on recalibrating global health by strengthening outcomes, education, clinical practice, and research. Attendees, including nurses, midwives, medical doctors, allied health professionals, academics, and government and non-government professionals, can share experiences and learn about health recalibration worldwide. The Scientific Committee is accepting abstracts for oral and poster presentations on themes such as administration, education, health promotion, quality of life, adult health, mental health, and other related topics that highlight health recalibration. The conference aims to discuss and update information on global health recalibration, including opportunities and challenges in strengthening health outcomes through interprofessional teamwork. It also seeks to provide a networking platform for healthcare professionals to contribute to advancing well-functioning cooperation and foster collaborations and partnerships with education, clinical practice, and research. Overall, Global Health Recalibration 2024 aims to improve health outcomes by strengthening education, clinical practice, and research, focusing on interprofessional healthcare teamwork. The conference offers a valuable opportunity for healthcare professionals to network and collaborate nationally and internationally towards promoting global health recalibration.
