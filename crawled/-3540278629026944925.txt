The event served as a platform for Dr. Chakkrapong Kuensaen to represent Chiang Mai University and promote a wide array of international programs, encompassing 17 bachelor's degree programs. The seminar attracted significant attention, drawing the interest of over 300 parents and high school students who were keen on pursuing higher education in Thailand. Distinguished representatives from Thailand's top five universities, including Chulalongkorn University, Mahidol University, Chiang Mai University, Kasetsart University, and Thammasat University, also actively participated in the event. The seminar was organized in collaboration with the Yunnan Around Asia International Education Center, located in Yunnan Province, People's Republic of China. Such collaborative efforts aim to foster educational exchanges and create opportunities for cross-cultural learning.
