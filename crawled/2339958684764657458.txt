Calories.\" 10 February 2023 International College of Digital Innovation The International College of Digital Innovation has organized the activity \"ICDI Mini Walk & Fun Run (Season 2)\" to exercise for happiness, \"Happy & Healthy Body: A Mission to Burn Calories.\" Under the project to promote the organization of happiness (Happy Workplace) for the fiscal year 2023, This activity aimed to encourage all college staff to get exercise, gain good physical and mental health, be happier, and make new friendships with staff within the college. Through running activities, there were 24 staff members participating in the activity, which has been divided into 3 people per team, for a total of 8 teams. The cumulative running time throughout the entire activity is 36 days. On Wednesday, February 8, 2023, Dr. Annop Thananchana, Director of the International Research and Academic Service Center, presided over the opening ceremony of the \"ICDI Mini Walk & Fun Run (Season 2)\" activity at the activity area, 1st floor, International College of Digital Innovation Building.
