A Gold Medal Prize was awarded to Mr. Peewawit Suriya, a fourth-year student in Mathematics, for his research titled ‘Submodularity Property for Facility Location of Dynamic Flow Networks’ supervised by Associate Professor Dr. Vorapong Suppakitpaisarn of The University of Tokyo and Assistant Professor Dr. Supanut Chaidee of the CMU Department of Mathematics.
