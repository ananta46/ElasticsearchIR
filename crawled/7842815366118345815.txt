Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) project for the year 2022 13 December 2022 International College of Digital Innovation International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, led by Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of Digital Innovation International College, along with the management team, faculty members, and supporting staff, supported 19 notebook computers for teaching and learning under the Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) project for the year 2022 on Friday, December 9, 2022, at Samakkhi San Muang School, Ban Chan Subdistrict, Kanlayaniwattana District, Chiang Mai Province. This event aims to help schools gain more access to the benefits of educational devices while also enhancing a good corporate image and fostering unity within the organization and society.
