Prof. Dr. Pichaya Poonlarp, Asst. Prof. Dr. Sorraya Khiewnavawongsa, Asst. Prof. Dr. Thanongsak Chaiyaso, and 4 Master students, applicants of the Japan Student Service Organization (JASSO) Scholarship 2021 attended a web conference with Prof. Dr. Masanori WATANABE from Yamagata University on Thursday 18 November, 2021. Prof. Dr. WATANABE explained the main reasons to delay their trip to Japan. In addition, information on students lodge and updated situation of Covid-19 were also discussed during the meeting. Nevertheless, JASSO students are scheduled to travel to Japan at the end of March 2022 to join this exchange programme for six months at the Faculty of Agriculture of Yamagata University. Yamagata University will urgently provide official acceptance letter and supporting documents to each student for preparation.
