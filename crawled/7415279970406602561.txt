The event aimed to provide support staff members with the opportunity to learn from internal units within the university that excel in continuous development and process improvement. The objectives of this activity were to allow the support staff of the college to learn from departments within the university that have best practices in organizational management. Additionally, it aimed to foster good relationships and companionships among the participants. A total of 29 staff members from the International College of Digital Innovation, CMU participated in this event.
