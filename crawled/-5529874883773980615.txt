Prof. Dr. Khongphu Nimanandh, Dean of Chiang Mai University Business School, together with the executive team joined a seminar with Prof. Dr. Bodo B. Schlegelmilch (Chair (& Trustee), International Management Board: AMBA (Association of MBAs) and BGA (Business Graduates Association) of United States to exchange concept and opinions in apply for International accreditation standards of Association at the 2nd floor meeting room CMUBS, for evaluation standards and ways for enhancing education quality, programs and working process of the faculty. In afternoon, CMUBS also organized a keynote session in a topic of “The Future of Management Education and the Role of Business School” to all faculty member by having Prof. Dr. Bodo B. Schlegelmilch as a speaker. The seminar was in hybrid format. The aim was to share his experiences in managing business school to be in accord with the contemporary global context.
