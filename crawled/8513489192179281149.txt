The Center for the Promotion of Arts and Culture has collected traditional houses as a Museum which consists of 10 houses and 4 rice granaries. The Center for the Promotion of Arts and Culture is currently improved to be a living Museum for the demonstration of Lanna life style, Lanna architecture and handicrafts as well as for academic purposes in Lanna studies and as a center of cultural activities.
