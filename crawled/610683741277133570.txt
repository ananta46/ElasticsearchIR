Associate Professor Dr. Wanicha Pungchompoo gave a report speech on the second year of the Healthy CMU project, which focused on the significance of health promotion among staff and collaboration building with other members of the Thai University Network for Health Promotion (TUN-HPN), wellness promotion organisations and related parties on the regional and national scales. The purpose was to enrich academic knowledge and enhance technological integration for effective and efficient health promotion and innovation, ultimately leading to a healthy organisation and a healthy society driven by healthy citizens. The event was held on April 27, 2023, at the Grand Ballroom, 3rd Floor, Duangtawan Hotel Chiang Mai.
