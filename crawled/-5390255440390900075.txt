The aim is to promote happiness through exercise, emphasizing a mission to conquer calories and achieve a happy and healthy body. This initiative is part of the project to foster happy organizations (Happy Workplace) for the fiscal year 2024. The goal is to encourage college personnel to engage in physical activity, promoting both their physical and mental well-being, fostering happiness, and building new friendships among the college staff through running activities. A total of 27 personnel participated in the event, forming 3 teams, with each team comprising 9 members. The running times were accumulated throughout the activity, which took place from November 24, 2024, to February 23, 2024, spanning a total of 90 days. On Friday, November 24, 2023, Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, the Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation, had the honor of chairing the opening ceremony for the \"ICDI Mini Walk & Fun Run #Season 3\" at the activity area on the 1st floor of ICDI building.
