Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News ICDI organized the seminar on the topic of \"U/sing the Electronic Document System at Chiang Mai University (CMU e-Document) for International College Digital Innovation administrators 24 January 2023 International College of Digital Innovation On January 20, 2023, Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation, together with the administrative team, welcomed Dr. Piyawan Chantrapanon, Secretary of the Faculty of Mass Communication, and a team of speakers from the Faculty of Mass Communication that were participating in the learning exchange seminar on the topic of \"Using the Electronic Document System at Chiang Mai University (CMU e-Document) for International College Digital Innovation administrators.\" This activity aims to exchange knowledge on strategies and policies for using the CMU e-Document system and driving the college management system into a digital organization. The dean has the policy to drive ICDI to become a Digital Transformation Organization, which aims to build all staff to have a digital mindset, develop digital talent, and develop a digital dashboard, which will lead to a digital culture in the organization. ICDI has a policy in place that requires all employees to use CMU e-Document by the budget year 2023.
