Faculty of Nursing, Chiang Mai University pleasure to invite you to the Global Health Recalibration conference, which will take place in Chiang Mai from September 4 to 6, 2024. With the conference theme \"Global Health Recalibration: Strengthening Outcomes, Education, Clinical Practice, and Research,\" aim to bring together academics, practitioners, researchers, and research students from worldwide to share experiences, successes, challenges, and lessons learned. . The conference will serve as an excellent interdisciplinary collaboration and knowledge-sharing platform to recalibrate global health systems by strengthening health outcomes, education, clinical practice, and research. Through insightful discussions and idea sharing, we aim to foster cooperation to achieve our common goals. .
