On Monday, January 23, 2023,  Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation, gave a congratulations speech to all graduates of the International College of Digital Innovation that attended Chiang Mai University's 57th commencement ceremony. This year, there were 5 graduates from ICDI receiving degrees: 4 Master of Science degrees and 1 Bachelor of Science degree. With a bachelor's degree, Ms. Jampal Dolma received a gold medal for academic excellence.
