Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University warmly welcomed delegations from Yunnan Vocational Institute of Energy Technology in the People's Republic of China. 13 December 2023 International College of Digital Innovation On Wednesday, December 13, 2023, International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, under the leadership of the Dean, Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, along with administrator and faculty members, warmly welcomed delegations from Yunnan Vocational Institute of Energy Technology in the People's Republic of China. This educational institution offers an exciting opportunity for our college community to embark on a journey to Qujing, People's Republic of China, on Saturday, August 19, 2023. The purpose of this trip is to engage in comprehensive studies across various fields, fostering mutual understanding and ensuring the continued collaboration between the two institutions. Notable attendees from Yunnan Vocational Institute of Energy Technology included Mr. DU Yunhang, Deputy Secretary, Mr. CAO Zhenyu, Director of Enrollment and Employment Department, Mr. HU Chun, Director of Finance Department, Mr. CHEN Chuan, Dean of Studies, Mr. DUAN Qibing, Dean of the School of Continuing Education, and Mr. ZHAO Jiang, Director of the Office of Science and Technology. This meeting served as a platform to explore and solidify collaborations between our institutions, laying the groundwork for future academic endeavors and knowledge exchange.
