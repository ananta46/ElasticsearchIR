and Development Agency (RTRDA) Ministry of Transport 24 March 2023 International College of Digital Innovation On March 23, 2023, Dr. Aniwat Phaphuangwittayakul, Dr. Saronsad Sokantika, and Ms. Jutharut Chatsirikul, lecturers from the International College of Digital Innovation (ICDI) Chiang Mai University received an invitation from Rail Technology Research and Development Agency (RTRDA) Ministry of Transport to be speakers and conduct a workshop on the topic “Business Intelligence (BI) and Data Model on Rail Technology.” Dr. Santi Charoenpornpattana, President, Dr. Pintip Vajarothai, Director of the Rail Technology Strategy Department, researchers, and more than ten RTRD experts attended and took part in the event. The purpose of this activity was to facilitate the exchange of knowledge and expertise among the participants in order to establish a Rail Technology Data Center and advance collaboration in academic research between the two institutions in order to build up and advance rail technology knowledge, innovation, standards, and personnel in Thailand.
