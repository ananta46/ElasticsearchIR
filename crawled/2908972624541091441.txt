This year, after COVID-19 relief, the Faculty of Social Sciences has rewarded 5 grants for 5 undergraduate student groups to go to Vietnam, Laos, Singapore, and Philippines. All the successful applicants are undergraduate students from all departments in the Faculty of Social Sciences (Geography, Sociology and Anthropology, Social Sciences (International program), and ASEAN Studies). After the projects finish, students must present their learning experiences during the project or Show & Share activities as the public seminar.
