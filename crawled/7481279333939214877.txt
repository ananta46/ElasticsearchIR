Prof. Dr. Somchai Jomduang, the Excellence in Silkworm and Silk in Non-Textile Industry award winner 12 September 2022 Faculty of Agro-Industry The Faculty of Agro-Industry would like to congratulate Asst. Prof. Dr. Somchai Jomduang, former lecturer of the faculty, who was given the Excellence in Sericulture Science Award 2022 in the categogy of  Silkwarm and Silk in Non-Textile Industry by the International Sericulture Commission. The award was presented in the 26th World Mulberry Academic Conference  during 7-11 September, 2022 at University of Agricultural Science and Veterinary Medicine, Cluj-Nacopa, Romania.
