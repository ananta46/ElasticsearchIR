Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News CMU-UT Symposium on Urban Engineering and Informatics 2024 24 April 2024 Corporate Communication and Alumni Relations Center (CCARC) Assistant Professor Todsaporn Pichaiya, Vice President, presided over the opening ceremony of the CMU-UT Symposium on Urban Engineering and Informatics 2024 with Associate Professor Thongchai Fongsamootr, Dean of the Faculty of Engineering, giving an overview report, as well as witnessing the signing of an MoU between the CMU Faculty of Engineering and the University of Tokyo, Japan, aiming to drive international collaborations with exchanges of knowledge, research and innovation for economic, social and environmental development. Taking place on April 24, 2024, in the Doi Suthep Meeting Room at Kantary Hill Chiang Mai, the symposium, held to promote collaboration and drive innovation, featured 16 presentations by scholars from both institutions with around 60 people in the audience.
