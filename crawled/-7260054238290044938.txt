On July 18, 2024, at Eastin Grand Hotel, Professor Dr. Pongruk Sribanditmongkol, CMU President, along with other university executives, attended the 15th ASEAN University Network Rectors themed ‘Shaping Our Region & The World: The Force of Universities in Sustainable Development’ and joined the panel discussion in Session 2 titled, ‘AUN Universities at the Forefront of Sustainable Development on the Global Stage.’  Dr. Choltis Dhirathiti, Managing Director of ASEAN University Network (AUN) presided over the opening ceremony, joined by executives of AUN network institutions. ?
