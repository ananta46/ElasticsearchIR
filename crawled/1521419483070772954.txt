Prof. Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, dean of the International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, along with the administrative team, have joined in welcoming Professor Ian Wooton, Vice Dean (international), from the University of Strathclyde, the United Kingdom, who visited the International College of Digital Innovation to discuss academic cooperation; a 2+2 double degree program at the undergraduate level and 1+1 at the postgraduate level between Strathclyde Business School and the International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, on Wednesday, November 16, 2022, at the International College of Digital Innovation. Additionally, on Thursday, November 17, 2022, Professor Ian Wooton also paid a visit to Assoc. Prof. Dr. Piyapong Niamsup, Assistant to the President of Chiang Mai University, at the Chiang Mai University Office and also met with students to give lectures and provide information about the University of Strathclyde at the International College of Digital Innovation afterward.
