Cerilles State College, Philippines, Visits Chiang Mai University to Discuss Academic and Sustainability Collaboration 13 December 2024 Smart Campus Management Center : SCMC Delegation from J.H. Cerilles State College, Philippines, Visits Chiang Mai University to Discuss Academic and Sustainability Collaboration On December 12, 2024, the Smart Campus Management Center at Chiang Mai University (CMU) warmly welcomed a delegation from J.H. Cerilles State College, Philippines, led by Dr. Edgardo H. Rosales, President of the institution. The visit aimed to explore opportunities for collaboration in research, academics, education, and benchmarking initiatives. During the visit, the Smart Campus Management Center hosted a reception and delivered a presentation on CMU’s development as a smart city, emphasizing the use of technology and innovation in resource management, service delivery, and environmental sustainability across all dimensions. This meeting and exchange of ideas highlighted opportunities to establish an international network of collaboration and marked the beginning of a long-term partnership focused on advancing education to address contemporary challenges. The visit from the J.H. Cerilles State College delegation reflects the commitment of both institutions to fostering strong relations and driving sustainable development together.
