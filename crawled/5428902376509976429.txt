‘Chiang Mai Designed at Will: Tackling Upcoming Air Pollution Crisis in 2025’ 30 July 2024 Corporate Communication and Alumni Relations Center (CCARC) Assistant to the President Paisit Panitkul was the moderator of a seminar titled ‘Chiang Mai Designed at Will: Tackling Upcoming Air Pollution Crisis in 2025’ held to promote an exchange of ideas and suggestions regarding numerous topics, including Chiang Mai as the pilot area for the Clean Air Act, updates on its progress and participation channels, approaches to fire and haze control by local actors, and public health measures to promote health security and ensure good health for the people of Chiang Mai. The event was held at Jing Jai Market, Chiang Mai, on July 20, 2024.
