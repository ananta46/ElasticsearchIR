Prof. Dr. Benjamas Suksatit, Acting  Dean for Student Development and International Relations of the International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, together with the delegation, were the representatives of the Buddhist Association of Thailand under the Royal Patronage of His Majesty the King, offering candles under the royal patronage of Somdej Phra Kanitthathirat Department of Her Royal Highness Princess Maha Chakri Sirindhorn Her Royal Highness Princess Maha Chakri Sirindhorn to Wat Suan Dok Phra Aram Luang, Suthep Subdistrict, Mueang District, Chiang Mai Province with royal grace Abbot of Wat Suan Dok Phra Aram Luang, together with the monks, received a candle that gave prayers to His Majesty.
