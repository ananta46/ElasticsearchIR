We are honored to have Assoc. Prof. Dr. Winita Punyodom, Vice President of Chiang Mai University, delivered the opening remarks, emphasizing the importance of international collaboration and the critical role of Big Data in transforming education and industry. The conference featured keynote speeches by Prof. Dr. Ralf Bornd?rfer from Freie Universit?t Berlin & Zuse-Institute Berlin, Germany, on “Achievements and Challenges in Railway Optimization,” and Prof. Dr. Thorsten Koch from TU-Berlin & Zuse-Institute Berlin, Germany, on “Integrating Large Citation Data Sets for Measuring Article’s Scientific Prestige.” There were also invited talks by experts from the Philippines, Malaysia, and Denmark. The oral presentations were available both on-site and online for international participants. Additionally, a welcome dinner was held at Khum Khantoke, where attendees enjoyed traditional Lanna cuisine and experienced local cultural traditions. The organizers expressed their gratitude to all participants and sponsors for contributing to the success of BDEE2024.
