Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News CMUBS Welcomes Prof. Dr. Erwin Rauch from Italy to Share Insights on “SMART AND SUSTAINABLE FACTORIES OF THE FUTURE” 23 July 2024 Chiang Mai University Business School On July 17, the Faculty of Business Administration at Chiang Mai University was honoured to host Prof. Dr. Erwin Rauch, an Endowed Professor for Smart and Sustainable Manufacturing at the Free University of Bolzano, Italy, for a special lecture titled \"SMART AND SUSTAINABLE FACTORIES OF THE FUTURE.\" Prof. Dr. Rauch presented valuable insights on trends, technologies, case studies, opportunities, and challenges in smart and sustainable manufacturing. His aim was to inspire students to integrate these concepts into their future careers. This lecture is a significant component of the SME 5.0 project, funded by the European Union’s Horizon 2021 Research and Innovation Program under the Marie Sk?odowska-Curie Grant agreement No. 101086487. Researchers from Chiang Mai University's Faculties of Engineering and Business Administration represent Thailand in this initiative to propel SMEs into the new 5.0 era. The objective is to enhance student learning and ignite their passion for future development. CMUBS is committed to producing quality graduates, enriching student learning through expert knowledge, and sharing real-world experiences.
