Venue : 08.30 – 11.30 at the 30th Anniversary Building, Faculty of Sciences, Chiang Mai University 13.30 – 16.30 at Research and Technology Transfer Building, Faculty of Engineering, Chiang Mai University ***Choose a timeslot for a COVID19 antigen test first and then pay for the fee before visiting the link for booking an appointment Pay for the fee (350 Baht) via a mobile banking application before booking an appointment for a COVID19 antigen test. Please transfer money to the following account  AMS Clinical Service Center Account Number 968-0000-990 Register for the COVID19 antigen test at http://cmu.to/5FgOP. Registration Password is 93084. The student must select either a morning timeslot from 08.30  - 11.30 or an afternoon timeslot from 13.30 – 16.30 only. (50 students per hour.) Please complete the registration information and attach the money transfer slip to confirm your COVID19 antigen test appointment. After completing registration and submitting the transfer slip, the student will receive a QR code via SMS or E-mail in order for them to show the staff at the testing venue. If you do not receive a QR Code please contact 064-3747513 during working hours. The COVID19 antigen test result will be sent to the student within 2 hours via SMS and via the Mohpromt Application. For appointment cancellations, the student needs to inform the AMS Clinical Service Center 7 days prior to the appointment date. However, the fee cannot be refunded. For further information, please contact Line @COVIDTESTCNX or Tel : 064-3747513 during working hours.
