e-Commerce 15 November 2023 International College of Digital Innovation ICDI-CMU Organized the Meeting to Discuss Academic Cooperation in Promoting Strong Communities through Online Marketing via Cross-Border e-Commerce On November 14, 2023, at 10:30 a.m., Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, the Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation, extended a warm welcome to Associate Professor Dr. Pradthana Jaiphong, the Director of School of Lifelong Education. The meeting, held at meeting room ICB1313,  International College of Digital Innovation, brought together administrators and personnel from the School of Lifelong Education. The primary agenda of the meeting was to deliberate on academic cooperation aimed at fostering robust communities through online marketing strategies, specifically within the realm of Cross-Border e-Commerce.
