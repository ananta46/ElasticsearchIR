September 2, 2022, via Zoom Meeting platform and Nurse KC Moodle Platform. Sixty health personnel from 18 countries registered for the course, including Bhutan, Burkina Faso, Costa Rica, Guinea, Indonesia, Iraq, Jordan, Kazakhstan, Maldivian, Mozambique, Myanmar, Nepal, Philippines, Saint Kitts and Nevis, Sri Lanka, Uganda, Vietnam, Yemen. Miss Hataichanok Siriwadhanakul, Human Resources Development Cooperation Division, Thailand International Cooperation Agency (TICA), delivered a closing speech, and Asst.  Prof. Dr.Thanee Kaewthummanukul, Ph.D., RN Dean, Faculty of Nursing, Chiang Mai University, gave the closing address to the participants.
