Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News The International College of Digital Innovation (ICDI), warmly welcomed a delegation from the International Education School of Chang'an University in Xi'an, Shaanxi Province, People’s Republic of China. 14 November 2024 International College of Digital Innovation The International College of Digital Innovation (ICDI), Chiang Mai University, led by Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation, along with faculty members, warmly welcomed a delegation from the International Education School of Chang'an University in Xi'an, Shaanxi Province, People’s Republic of China, a partner university of Chiang Mai University. The delegation included: 1. Mr. Lyu Weidong (Deputy Dean of the International Education School) 2. Ms. Zhang Lei (Director of the Admissions Office of the International Education School) 3. Ms. He Mengchan (Program Manager of Admissions) The purpose of this visit was to continue strengthening academic collaboration following the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding between Chang'an University and Chiang Mai University in June. During this visit, the delegation from the International Education School engaged in discussions with the International College of Digital Innovation on various opportunities for academic cooperation, including joint curriculum development, collaborative research, and both short-term and long-term student exchange programs. The delegation visited ICDI on Thursday, November 14, 2024, at meeting room ICB1313, located on the 3rd floor of the International College of Digital Innovation building.
