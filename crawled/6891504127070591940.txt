The competition involved 35 teams representing 12 esteemed institutions across the nation, including Chulalongkorn University, Thammasat University, Mahidol University International College (MUIC), Kasetsart University (Bang Khen), Kasetsart University (Sri Racha), Mae Fah Luang University, Mae Jo University, Prince of Songkhla University, Siam Technology College, Silapakorn University, Shinawatra University, and Chiang Mai University. The victorious Team NBTYM featured three students from CMUBS, claiming the first-place position, while Team Nihao, with one CMUBS member, secured the second-place position. The results were as follows: The winner:  Team NBTYM from Chiang Mai University (CMUBS) First runner-up:  Team Nihao from Chulalongkorn University and Chiang Mai University (CMUBS) Second runner-up:  Team Black Pearl from Kasetsart University Three honorable mentions prizes:  Team Miracle from Mae Jo University, Team Frontier from Kasetsart University (Bang Khen) and Team FIVEFORCES from Kasetsart University (Sri Racha).
