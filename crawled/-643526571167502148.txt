The activities included health screenings, self-care advice, exercise sessions, and a special lecture on Dengue Hemorrhagic Fever Prevention. Additionally, there was a workshop on making herbal mosquito repellent sachets to educate the elderly on preventing mosquito bites and mosquito-borne diseases. . This event was part of the students' clinical practice in internal medicine, focusing on applying their knowledge to promote the health of the elderly. The hands-on activities provided a valuable learning experience that also contributed to enhancing the quality of life and health of the senior citizens.
