The event was held on Thursday, January 23, 2025, at the Lanna Traditional House Museum, Chiang Mai University. . On this occasion, the Faculty of Nursing had the honor of hosting and welcoming representatives from Taipei Medical University (Chinese Taipei) and Asahi University (Japan). This engagement was part of the academic symposium Presidential Forum, under the theme Navigating the Future of Higher Education. . The forum was organized to mark the special occasion of the 60th Anniversary Celebration of CMU, providing a platform for educational leaders to exchange ideas and shape the future direction of higher education on a global scale. This event highlights Chiang Mai University's role as a leading educational institution committed to fostering international collaborations, promoting academic excellence, and building sustainable global relationships.
