USA. 3 July 2024 Corporate Communication and Alumni Relations Center (CCARC) On Wednesday, 3rd July 2024, Professor of Practice Ekkachai Mahaek, Ph.D., Vice president, along with executives from Faculty of Fine Arts extended a warm welcome to Professor Ken Ueno, a distinguished faculty member and Professor of Music at the University of California, Berkeley (UCB). Professor Ueno visited Chiang Mai University (CMU) to discuss potential academic collaborations between UCB and CMU, particularly focusing on the field of music. The meeting took place at the Office of the University.
