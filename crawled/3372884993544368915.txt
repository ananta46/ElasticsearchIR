The students attended a special lecture that covered the operations and business strategy of Chiang Mai Night Safari, including how it has managed through various crises in the past. They also had the opportunity to enjoy a close-up tram ride to observe the animals and witness the highlight of the safari—a live show featuring lions and tigers. They enjoyed the learning experience at Chiang Mai Night Safari, where they gained an understanding of the importance of wildlife and nature conservation. Additionally, the visit offered them an example of internationally recognized best practices in tourism service management. The event helped to create positive memories and foster strong relationships among the students, which will be beneficial for developing teamwork skills in the future.
