University Enterprise Assistance Association in Xi'an, Shaanxi Province, People's Republic of China 21 June 2024 International College of Digital Innovation International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, Signed an Academic Cooperation Agreement with the Xi'an Peking University Enterprise Assistance Association in Xi'an, Shaanxi Province, People's Republic of China On June 11, 2024, from 3:00 to 5:00 PM, Assistant Professor Todsaporn Pichaiya, Vice President of Chiang Mai University, led a delegation from the International College of Digital Innovation and the Faculty of Engineering to visit the Xi'an Peking University Enterprise Assistance Association. The purpose of the visit was to sign an academic cooperation agreement between the two institutions. The association is renowned for its expertise in educational guidance and cooperative education, emphasizing the promotion and support of sending Chinese students to study abroad. The signing ceremony was followed by a productive discussion exploring potential collaborative opportunities between the two institutions. The CMU delegation included: 1. Asst. Prof. Todsaporn Pichaiya, Vice President of Chiang Mai University 2. Asst. Prof. Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of International College of Digital Innovation 3. Asst. Prof. Dr. Benjamas Suksatit, Deputy Dean of International College of Digital Innovation 4. Asst. Prof. Dr. Korrakot Yaibuathet Tippayawong, Assistant to the Dean and Director of Chiang Mai International Engineering School (CM-IES), Faculty of Engineering 5. Dr. Tanarat Rattanadamrongaksorn, Lecturer at International College of Digital Innovation 6. Dr. Aniwat Phaphuangwittayakul, Lecturer at International College of Digital Innovation 7. Mr. Attapon Tipsribud, Student Quality Development Officer of International College of Digital Innovation, and Interpreter 8. Ms. Thanyaphorn Sriwichailamphan, International Relations Officers of International College of Digital Innovation 9. Ms. Usanee Pongsawaleesri, Secretary of Chiang Mai International Engineering School (CM-IES), Faculty of Engineering 10. Mr. Jinming Zhang, Research Assistant at International College of Digital Innovation The delegation from the Xi'an Peking University Enterprise Assistance Association included: 11. Mr. Wang Haizhou, President of Xi'an Peking University Enterprise Assistance Association 12. Mr. Xu Kaisheng, Secretary General of Xi'an Peking University Enterprise Assistance Association 13. Mr. Wang Xi, Vice President of Xi'an Peking University Enterprise Assistance Association and Head of China-Thailand Cross Border E-Commerce (Northwest China) 14. Ms. Zhao Ping, Executive Secretary General and Office Director of Xi'an Peking University Enterprise Assistance Association 15. Mr. Wei Wei, Director of the Project Incubation Department of Xi'an Peking University Enterprise Assistance Association 16. Ms. Xu Rong, Director of the International Exchange Center of Xi'an Peking University Enterprise Assistance Association 17. Mr. Chen Shuming, Vice Minister of Network Media Department of Xi'an Peking University Enterprise Assistance Association 18. Mr. Yang Yong, Deputy Secretary General of Xi'an Peking University Enterprise Assistance Association 19. Mr. Ning Yuguo, Assistant to the President of Xi'an Peking University Enterprise Assistance Association 20. Mr. Zhang Didi, Secretary to the President of Xi'an Peking University Enterprise Assistance Association During the meeting, both parties discussed the maximum support that each institution is willing to provide to ensure the success of the collaboration. Additionally, they explored the recruitment of high-quality students for 17 international programs at Chiang Mai University, with the Xi'an Peking University Enterprise Assistance Association committing to assist in identifying and recommending outstanding candidates. This meeting highlighted the mutual interests and goals of both institutions, laying the groundwork for future collaborations in various fields, including academic exchanges, joint research projects, and cross-border e-commerce initiatives.
