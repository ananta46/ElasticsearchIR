August 10, 2024 - Special Excursion Before the workshop, participants from Feng Chia University (FCU), Japan Woman’s University (JWU), Tianjin University (TJU), and Tamkang University (TKU) were treated to a special excursion, visiting significant sites such as: Wat Ton Kwan Lanna Rice Barn Maiiam Contemporary Art Museum Weave Artisan Society Wua Lai Saturday Walking Street We extend our heartfelt thanks to Adul Heranya and Julian Huang for their warm hospitality at Lanna Rice Barn and Weave Artisan Society, and to Professor Worrasit Tantinipankul for guiding us through Wat Ton Kwan. August 11, 2024 - Continued Excursion The special excursion continued for participants from FCU, JWU, TJU, and TKU, including visits to: Chiang Mai City Tour by Greenery.Beauty.Scent Baan Suan Residence Kalm Village Chiang Mai Sunday Walking Street We are grateful to Baan Suan Residence and Kalm Village for their warm welcome. August 12, 2024 - Workshop Day 1 The day began with the official opening ceremony of the ASIAN Housing Workshop, with participation from Chiang Mai University, FCU, JWU, TJU, and TKU. This was followed by a briefing on the project \"New Public Space and Prototype of Housing for the Mae-Kha Canal Community,\" which aims to redevelop public spaces and housing for the Mae-Kha Canal community, supported by the Chiang Mai municipality. Special thanks to Jai Baan Studio and Mae-Kha City Lab for their insightful presentations and guidance. In the afternoon, participants visited Zone 4 of the Mae-Kha Canal for a site visit and brainstorming session at the ARCHCMU VDO Wall. We appreciate the support from Mae-Kha City Lab and Warong Wonglangka, Lectuerer of Landscape Architecture Program for facilitating the site visit. August 14, 2024 - Special Lectures Special lectures were delivered by professors from JWU, TKU, FCU, and TJU, followed by students presenting their schematic designs in the afternoon. August 15, 2024 - Mid-Workshop Excursion Participants were taken on an exclusive tour to: Chiang Mai Heritage Center Association of Siamese Architects (ASA) Northern Chapter CMU Lanna House Museum Chiang Mai University Demonstration School Wat Pha Lat We sincerely thank the Chiang Mai Heritage Center, ASA Northern Chapter, CMU Lanna House Museum, Chiang Mai University Demonstration School, and Wat Pha Lat for their warm hospitality. August 17, 2024 - Final Presentation Day The workshop concluded with creative design presentations from each group, followed by a celebratory moment. We extend our deepest gratitude to the students and faculty from Chiang Mai University’s Integrated Design in Emerging Architecture program (IDEA), FCU, JWU, TJU, and TKU for their participation. Lastly, a special thanks to Mishima Sensei for providing valuable feedback, and to our organizing team—Pla Pareena, Qian Rui, Prem, and Amm—for making this event a success. See you all again soon!
