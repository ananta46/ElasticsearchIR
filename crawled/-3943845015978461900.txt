On the same occasion, the Vice President attended the signing of an MoU on advancing the project with the Chiang Mai Provincial Education Office and 25 other provincial agencies with Vice Governor Worawit Chaisawat presiding over the ceremony. The Credit Bank project aims to foster human resource development in line with the National Strategy BE 2561 – 2580 (2018 - 2037) through a systematic and effective lifelong learning system, the National Credit Bank, which will allow the Thai workforce to upskill and reskill, leading to professional improvement.
