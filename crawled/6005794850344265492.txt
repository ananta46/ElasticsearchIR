This event aimed to provide crucial information about universities and colleges while fostering good relationships between parents, students, and faculty members. Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Uncharoen, the Dean of the college, warmly welcomed the students and parents, while Assistant Professor Dr. Benjamas Suksathit, the Associate Dean, provided an overview of extracurricular activities, scholarships, and visas for foreign students. Lecturer Dr. Chakkrapong Kuensaen discussed teaching and learning, including the use of teaching aids. The event also featured sub-meetings introducing first-year student advisors, catering to both Thai and international students. The event took place at Chiang Mai University's UNISERV, Inthanin Conference Room, and was accessible online via Zoom. This informative gathering on June 12, 2023, facilitated collaboration and knowledge sharing, ensuring that participants clearly understood the university's offerings and available resources.
