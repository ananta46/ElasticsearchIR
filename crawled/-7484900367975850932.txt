The aim of the course is to prepare the high-level HEI staff that will undertake the lead role in the setup and management of the ASTRA R&D Hubs in Laos and Thailand. It will include presentations, workshops, study and cultural visits, and interactive activities. Bringing together expertise from computer science and social science can help us to better understand the forces behind these research hub of Astra project outcomes. This partnership will help partners of the consortium come from Germany, Greece, Italy, Laos, and Thailand to set up, organization and operation of four new Research and Development Innovation Hubs (2 in Laos and 2 in Thailand) CMU and ASTRA consortium will convene for research proposal preparation of the Erasmus+ meetings to highlight progress on key research issues and to explore new opportunities to work together on future research agendas during 12-26 November 2022 at the Athens University of Economics and Business Athens Greece. We see collaboration and cooperation as the future, and we can keep in touch and look forward to future collaboration.
