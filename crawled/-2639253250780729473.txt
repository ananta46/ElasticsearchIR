On September 20, 2024, the Faculty of Architecture, Chiang Mai University, held the second day of the Bamboo Workshop in celebration of the 15th World Bamboo Day. Participants applied the skills they had learned on the first day, including tool usage, bamboo construction, and bamboo architectural design, to create models based on their own ideas. They used their creativity to design and present their work in model form. The workshop was conducted under the supervision of the Creative Lanna Innovation Center (CLIC).
