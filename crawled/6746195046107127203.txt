Their visit, spanning from June 5th to 9th, 2023, was greeted with warm hospitality extended by Prof. Yves Schoonjans, the Dean of the Faculty of Architecture, KU Leuven, and Prof. Koen De Wandeler, accompanied by the executive team. During their visit, they had the opportunity to explore both campuses of KU Leuven, namely the Brussels Campus and Gent Campus. Notably, they attended the captivating presentations of seven master's theses, gaining valuable insights. Furthermore, they engaged in fruitful discussions to forge collaborations for the upcoming joint Summer School project in the next academic year. This activity is a part of the One Faculty One MoU Project (2nd round) under the Quick Win Project, Plan 13 Policy, Chiang Mai University. The aim of this collaboration is to encourage, promote, and support academic collaborations with existing or new partners, creating a mutually beneficial exchange of knowledge, research, and innovation for students and staff.
