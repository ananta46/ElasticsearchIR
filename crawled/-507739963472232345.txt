The workshop was honored by the presence of Pol. Lt. Wattanat Amornsirihiran, Pol. Sgt. Sawatch Wiangjai, and Pol. Sgt. Phurinat Phan-ngamkruea, lecturers from the Special Operations Division, Investigation Division, Provincial Police Region 5, Chiang Mai Province. They delivered a lecture on the topic of “Situations Involving Armed Assailants” and led a simulation exercise on “Active Shooter: Run, Hide, and Fight.” The purpose of the workshop was to equip participants with essential knowledge and skills for survival in active shooter scenarios. The training provided both theoretical insights and practical experience in handling such emergencies, helping participants develop the skills necessary to protect life and property. A total of 40 participants attended the workshop.
