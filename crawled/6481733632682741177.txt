Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News The executive and faculty members from ICDI, Chiang Mai University, met with students from the International College of Digital Innovation who are participating in the dual degree program between the Digital Innovation (International Program) at the International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, and the International Economics and Trade program at the School of Business, East China University of Science and Technology (ECUST) in Shanghai, People's Republic of China.\" 29 October 2024 International College of Digital Innovation On Sunday, October 27, 2024, the International College of Digital Innovation (ICDI), Chiang Mai University, led by Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation, along with the delegations, visited East China University of Science and Technology (ECUST) in Shanghai, People's Republic of China, to meet with students from the International College of Digital Innovation participating in the 2+2 double degree program between the Digital Innovation Program (International Program) of ICDI, CMU, and the International Economics and Trade Program, School of Business, ECUST. The four participating students are: 1. Ms. Anpanote Kitsamakitkul 2. Ms. Panicha Kiattirakul 3. Ms. Panisara Sirima 4. Mr. Chaloemwut Silphatirathorn This meeting provided an opportunity for students to share their experiences from the program and offer preparation tips to younger students interested in joining. The double degree program is significant as it allows students to earn bachelor's degrees from both institutions within just four years—two years at ICDI and two years at ECUST. In addition to shortening the study period, this program helps students develop global citizenship skills and enhances their future business opportunities in both countries.
