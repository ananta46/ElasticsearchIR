Go to Website Navigation About Organizations Executives Services Contact Home Organization Multidisciplinary Research Institute (MDRI) About Offices Multidisciplinary Research Institute (MDRI) Chiang Mai University was established on February 25, 2023, from the combining of Science and Technology Research Institute (STRI) and Social Research Institute (SRI). This was officially announced by the Royal Thai government gazette on the 25th of February 2023 B.E. MDRI is the only institute in CMU which contributes research based on science & technology research and social research and aims to deliver high potential, high social impact research to the community. Vision An excellent institute in multidisciplinary research and academic services, also aiming to create tangible knowledge recognized at an international level. Mission Promote and support research & academic services for beneficiary utilization along with providing high level publication on a national and international level. Develop a new generation of researchers with multidisciplinary knowledge & abilities. Develop research that has a high impact in social, economic, and environmental contexts. Organization Culture M = Morality D = Driving for Excellence R = Resilience I = Integration Values Creative thinking, meet the needs of research questions, provide attentive service, create social value.
