The seminar topic is \"Developing a 5-year Strategic Plan of International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University (2023–2027).\" The objective is to enable administrators, academic staff, and supporting staff to understand the context, direction, and policy of ICDI according to the CMU Educational Development Plan, Phase 13 (2023–2027) to jointly brainstorm ideas and analyze the performance and situation of higher education both in Thailand and abroad, including the change of world social trends in many dimensions, for use as information in the preparation of the Strategic Plan of the ICDI for 5 years (2023–2027) and to give administrators, academic staff, and supporting staff more opportunities to meet, discuss, and exchange ideas with each other. This will help create an atmosphere of cooperation in driving and developing ICDI as an organization, to be able to support and keep up with the changing context of the world situation and drive the operation to achieve the goals set.
