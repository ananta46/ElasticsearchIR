Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation participated in the 7th International ZIBIMI-ISM-NUS-RIKEN-MODAL-NHR workshop titled \"Future Algorithms and Applications\" held at the Zuse Institute Berlin in Berlin, Germany 9 October 2023 International College of Digital Innovation Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation, along with Lecturer Dr. Piang-or Loahavilai and PhD student Ms. Zhai Fan, participated in the 7th International ZIBIMI-ISM-NUS-RIKEN-MODAL-NHR workshop titled \"Future Algorithms and Applications\" held at the Zuse Institute Berlin in Berlin, Germany, from September 25 to September 30, 2023. This 7th workshop featured presentations and the exchange of innovative ideas and new algorithms, as well as ongoing developments in applied mathematics research applications. Moreover, it facilitated collaboration and knowledge sharing among relevant institutions and industries.
