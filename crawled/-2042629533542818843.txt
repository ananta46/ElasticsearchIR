activity called \"Closing Ceremony of International Buddy Program\" 12 September 2022 International College of Digital Innovation The Student Quality Development Unit of the International College of Digital Innovation at Chiang Mai University has organized an activity called \"Closing Ceremony of International Buddy Program,\" which is an activity that encourages both Thai and international students to build good relations with each other. The activity provides opportunities for international students and Thai students to engage in activities that focus on cross-cultural learning in order to appreciate cultural diversity and promote an international atmosphere. It also emphasizes communication and practicing foreign language skills throughout to develop the quality and international competence of students. The Closing Ceremony emphasizes how important these activities are for the students who have been through them. A total of 74 students and staff participated on September 9, 2022, at the International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University.
