The meeting, which took place from 10:00 a.m. to 12.00 p.m. ,focused on advancing various collaborative initiatives between the two institutions. The meeting was attended by key figures from ICDI, including Asst. Prof. Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of international college of digital innovation, and Assoc. Prof. Dr. Somchai Sriyab, Associate Dean of international college of digital innovation. Their leadership and contributions were pivotal in steering the discussions and aligning the strategies of both institutions. Key participants from Chengdu University included Assoc. Prof. Dr. Mu Lei, Deputy Director of the Office of International Cooperation and Exchange; Prof. Dr. Yu Xi, Dean of Stirling College; and Assoc. Prof. Dr. Fu Jing, Assistant Dean of the School of Tourism and Cultural Industry. Their active involvement and valuable insights were instrumental in shaping the outcomes of the discussions. During the meeting, several important topics were discussed, including monthly student progress meet-ups, the MOU between Chiang Mai University and Chengdu University, the MOA of the Sister City Center, and plans for graduate study short courses. The discussions were productive and set the stage for continued cooperation. The International College of Digital Innovation looks forward to the continued partnership with Chengdu University as both work together to achieve shared goals in education and international cooperation.
