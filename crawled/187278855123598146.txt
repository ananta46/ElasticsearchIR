Miss Hataichanok Siriwadhanakul, Human Resources Development Cooperation Division, Thailand International Cooperation Agency (TICA) and Asst. Prof. Dr. Thanee Kaewthummanukul, Ph.D., RN Dean, Faculty of Nursing, Chiang Mai University, gave welcome speeches to the participants. Asst. Prof. Dr. Rangsiya Narin oversees this training and gave an orientation to the health and public health personnel from various countries. The training is held from 19 - 30 September 2022 via Zoom Meeting and Online Classroom System (NurseCMU KC Moodle) for 68 students from 23 countries including Azerbaijan, Bhutan, Chile, Costa Rica, Ecuador, Guinea, India, Indonesia, Iraq, Kazakhstan, Madagascar, Maldives, Mali, Morocco, Mozambique, Nepal, Nigeria, Oman, Pakistan, Panama, Philippines, Sri Lanka, and Tanzania.
