This visit was supported by the British Embassy in Bangkok. The purpose of the visit was to engage in discussions regarding academic collaboration, particularly focusing on potential study abroad programs, as well as student and staff exchange initiatives. The visit was facilitated by Mr. Steve Waterworth, the Head of International Partnerships at Staffordshire University's International Recruitment & Development department. During the visit, ICDI had the valuable opportunity to discuss various avenues for collaboration and partnership, while fostering international cooperation and providing enriching opportunities for students and staff. This visit sets the stage for future academic collaborations, facilitating the exchange of knowledge, experiences, and cultural understanding.
