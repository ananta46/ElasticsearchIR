The Loy Krathong Festival usually takes place in November of every year. The event was a workshop held in 2 sessions. The morning session was a workshop for the paper lantern, also known as \"Khomfai,\" and the afternoon session was the Krathong-making workshop. The event was held at the International College of Digital Innovation Building from 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. on Monday, November 7, 2022.
