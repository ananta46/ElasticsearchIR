The event was honored by the presence of Associate Professor Dr. Usanee Kumprakrob, Vice President of Chiang Mai University, who presided over the opening of the seminar and delivered a lecture on the topic \"Guidelines for the Implementation of International and Bilingual Undergraduate Programs for the 2025 Fiscal Year.\" The seminar also included a discussion on challenges, obstacles, and suggestions related to the management and promotion of international and bilingual programs at the undergraduate level, led by Associate Professor Dr. Manad Khamkong, Deputy Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation, and Assistant Professor Dr. Benjamas Suksatit. Additionally, there was a lecture on \"Guidelines for Admissions to International Programs for the 2025 Academic Year\" by Associate Professor Dr. Piyaluk Buddhawongsa, Deputy Director of  the Registration Office. The seminar aimed to provide a platform for administrators, faculty members, and staff from various faculties and colleges at Chiang Mai University that offer international and bilingual undergraduate programs to exchange ideas on program management, educational administration, and public relations. Additionally, it sought to promote collaboration by fostering good relationships among staff members and creating opportunities to discuss and address future challenges related to program management. This exchange of ideas and experiences is expected to contribute to the continuous improvement and development of international and bilingual programs, ensuring they meet high academic standards and effectively address the needs of students and stakeholders in the future.
