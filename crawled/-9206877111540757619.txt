Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News CAMT study field trips for KIM master students in Academic Year 2023 10 April 2024 College of Art, Media  and Technology Knowledge and Innovation Management (KIM) Program, College of Arts, Media and Technology Chiang Mai University led by Asst.Prof.Dr.Acrapol Nimmolrat, Head Department of KIM, and two lecturers of KIM, who are Asst.Prof.Dr.Chalermpon Kongjit and Dr.Wantana Areeprayolkij had a schedule for the study field trips for KIM master students at Sustainable Manufacturing Center: SMC in Rayong province to explore technology on the Smart Factory hosted by SMC, National Electronics and Computer Technology Center (NECTEC), The Eastern Economic Corridor of Innovation (EECi). The primary learning topics were conducted by Pornprom Ateetanan, Ph.D., the Deputy division director, Strategic planning and partner development division, covering SMC and EECi introduction, Industrial IoT and Data Analytics Platforms, utility systems, and tools in intelligent manufacturing. This visit aimed to exchange experiences with the organization's support, strengthen potential in Knowledge and Innovation Management, as well as enhancing students’ proficiency. Moreover, the team also visited the labs and showcases and attended student workshops on the Knowledge Capture and Application of SMC’s Innovative Learning at A-One the Royal Cruise Hotel, Pattaya, on 4-5 April 2024.
