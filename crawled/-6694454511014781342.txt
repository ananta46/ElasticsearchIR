Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News AMS and InnoFab Staff Named Second Runner-up at MEDCHIC Health Hackathon 2022 11 August 2022 Faculty of Associated Medical Sciences Three lecturers from the CMU Faculty of Associated Medical Sciences, Assistant Professor Dr. Siriphan Kongsawasdi, Assistant Professor Dr. Kittichai Wantanajittikul and Dr. Busaba Chuatrakoon, in collaboration with Associate Professor Dr. Radom Pongvuthitham, Assistant Professor Dr. Wetchayan Rangsri and Dr. Faifan Tantakitti from the Innovation Fabrication (InnoFab) Lab, CMU Faculty of Engineering, were named the second runner-up at the MEDCHIC Health Hackathon 2022 for their project titled Boon Chuay, which is a set of harness equipment that assists patients with mobility impairment during their walking and standing training.  The event was held by the Suandok Medical Innovation District at Kantary Hills Hotel, Chiang Mai from August 6 – 7, 2022.
