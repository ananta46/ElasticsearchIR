The University’s Presidents and Deans signed the Collaboration Agreement file together and gave the promise that after the ceremony, both universities will implement an exchange of undergraduate (and graduate) students, develop collaborative research projects, and exchange research and teaching personnel, among other matters on the 7th of April, 2022, via the Zoom Meeting platform.
