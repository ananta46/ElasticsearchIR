The seminar aims to summarize the performance of ICDI in the past 5 years (2018-2022). The Dean, Assistant Prof. Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, also presented the agenda for the year 2022-2026 to all ICDI staff, which promotes an organizational communication culture, increase all staff engagement, and provides opportunities for executives and staff under ICDI to meet and create the organizational bond. In addition, there was also an \"Award Ceremony\" for ICDI members for the year 2022, including 4 ICDI Awards 2022 and 8 STAR Awards 2022, awarded by executives. A total of 62 ICDI members participated in this seminar.
