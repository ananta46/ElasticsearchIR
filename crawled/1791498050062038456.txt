This program, organized by the AUN Secretariat in collaboration with Nanyang Technological University and the National University of Singapore, featured 40 student representatives from 22 ASEAN university networks. The program aimed to enhance cultural experiences and knowledge sharing on issues affecting the ASEAN region, with the theme \"SMART NATION: TRANSFORMING OUR NATIONS THROUGH TECHNOLOGY.\" Ms. Petchpraw Somjit received the Best Presentation award for her innovative technology-driven solutions to social problems. Additionally, Lect. Dr. Worawit Tepsan, who accompanied the students on this trip, explored collaborations with Nanyang Technological University, Mahidol University, and Vietnam National University to organize social service projects for students, promoting participation and understanding in ASEAN society. Exciting joint activities are anticipated in the future as we continue to foster cooperation and engagement within the ASEAN community.
