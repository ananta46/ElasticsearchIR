Services will be available on the 1st and 2nd floors of Building 1. Service details are as follows: 1. Study Space Services Available areas: 1st and 2nd floors of Library Building 1 only 2. Resource Borrowing-Returning Services 2.1 Book borrowing and returning after 21:00: No staff available. Users can borrow books via Self-Check machines or CMU Mobile 2.2 Electronic equipment can be borrowed before 21:00 3. Eligible Users 3.1 Chiang Mai University staff and students: Access via CMU Mobile or registered staff/student ID cards 3.2 Library members categories A, B, C, D with membership cards: Access via card scanning 3.3 General public: Entry fee 20 baht, no access permitted after 21:00 4. Security Measures Building entry-exit requires fingerprint scanning for record-keeping and monitoring of 24-hour room users. Security personnel will conduct regular patrols throughout the 24-hour period. Note: Complimentary refreshments will be provided to library users during the 24-hour service period from January 2-8, 2025, as part of the library's image enhancement and user engagement activities.
