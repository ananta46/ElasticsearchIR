Professor Prasert Rerkkriengkrai, Vice President, presided over the meeting on carbon neutrality which is an agenda in CMU Plan 13 to address the global warming crisis by creating models that reduce greenhouse gases emissions and promote issues such as renewable energy, energy and environmental conservation, forest, waste reduction, transportation, eco-friendly innovation, participation in eco-friendly activities, innovation and research that reduce costs and risks from natural disasters, social responsibility. It will also serve as a national model in reducing carbon emission which is responsible by the subcommittee for advancing carbon neutrality targets and developing a comprehensive carbon reduction plan. The meeting was held at the Office of the University’s Phraya Srivisarnvacha Room on July 15, 20233.
