Both sets of students practiced discussing ideas to develop technology in nursing care in order to make it more effective, as well as presenting data jointly. The activity was held via the Zoom Meeting Platform on Wednesday, June 7th, 2023. The seminar was attended by three nursing students from each side, as well as 25 observers.
