Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News The International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University organized \"Fire Training Workshop and Fire Evacuation Drills for the Year 2022 7 September 2022 International College of Digital Innovation The International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, with Ms. Pantinee Nestsupaluk, Secretary of ICDI, presided over the opening speech of the \"Fire Training Workshop and Fire Evacuation Drills for the Year 2022,\" which were honored by guest speakers Mr. Supaphon Phonyiam, Head of the Emergency Response Team (ERT), and Mr. Pongthorn Bunchomchai, ERT member of the Chiang Mai Provincial Disaster Prevention and Mitigation Office. The training included the basic knowledge and understanding of the use of firefighting equipment, as well as the ability to use fire extinguishing equipment correctly and efficiently. The training also provided ICDI staff with knowledge and understanding of ways to prevent and react in the event of a fire and the concept of surviving a fire. The speakers also conducted fire evacuation drills by simulating the danger of fire and proper ways to take action to help fire victims. The training was held on Wednesday, September 7, 2022, at Room ICB1102, International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, and 35 ICDI staff participated in the training.
