The aim of the event was to provide students with knowledge about the history, traditions, and culture of the region, while also giving them the opportunity to visit significant landmarks in Lampang and Lamphun provinces. During the trip, students visited several important sites, such as the Thai Elephant Conservation Center, the Kad Kong Ta Cultural Road, and Wat Phra That Doi Phra Chan in Lampang, as well as Wat Phra That Hariphunchai Woramahawihan in Lamphun. Additionally, students participated in a workshop and visited the Dhanabadee Ceramic Museum in Lampang.
