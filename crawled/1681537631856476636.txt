Prof. Dr. Soraya Khiewnavawongsa attended the Workshop SECRA (ERASMUS + Project: Strengthening University-Enterprise Collaboration Communities in Asia) with university partners from UK, Sri Lanka, Estronia, the Philippines. The workshop, hosted by Naresuan University, will be held in Phitsanulok, Thailand from 6 to 10 February, 2023. In the late afternoon, delegates from CMU and other country members visited Unit of Startup and Innovation Services (USIS) of the Faculty of Engineering whose main objective of the Unit is to provide services to Industrial groups in the Lower North of Thailand who needs to create new innovations and also paid a visit to Food Innopolis at the Faculty of Agriculture, Natural Resources and Environment.
