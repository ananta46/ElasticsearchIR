There are more than 18 students participating in the activity, including students from the Faculty of Humanities, Faculty of Nursing, Faculty of Business Administration, Faculty of Social Sciences, Faculty of Political Science, Faculty of Science, and the International College of Digital Innovation. This activity is part of the CEFR and 21st Century Skills Project, hosted by Foreign Language Expert (Expat), Ms. Sarah Seymour. This project, which includes workshops on writing English as well as games and movies, is still ongoing and will run throughout the second semester of 2022.
