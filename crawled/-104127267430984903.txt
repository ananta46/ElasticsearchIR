YVIET is a multidisciplinary vocational college renowned for its expertise in six areas: science, technology, economics, management, humanities, and arts. It is recognized as a premier institution excelling in the fields of science and technology. The purpose of the visit was to engage in negotiations aimed at fostering cooperation and establishing a partnership between the Digital Innovation International College and YVIET. This collaboration seeks to facilitate academic and student exchanges in the future, enabling knowledge sharing and enhancing the educational experience for both institutions.
