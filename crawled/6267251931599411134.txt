Pongruk Sribanditmongkol, CMU President and Chair of the Council of University Presidents of Thailand (CUPT), and Assistant Professor Dr. Tanyanuparb Anantana, CMU Vice President and Chair of the International Missions Committee, attended the signing of an MoU between CUPT and the Association of Southeast Asian Institutions of Higher Learning (ASAIHL) to promote collaboration in research and innovation between students and academic staff of Thai and Canadian institutions. Held at the MR211 AB Room, Lakeside Zone, Floor 2, Queen Sirikit National Convention Centre on July 24, 2024, the ceremony was attended by presidents and representatives of universities nationwide.
