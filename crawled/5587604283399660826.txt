The event marked the joyous occasion of the 58th Royal Graduation Ceremony at Chiang Mai University. This year, ICDI proudly witnessed 15 graduates receiving their degrees. The degrees were distributed as follows: 10 graduates earned a Bachelor of Science degree, 5 achieved a Master of Science degree, and 1 earned a Doctor of Philosophy degree. Notably, 5 graduates were honored with first-class distinctions in academics, while 1 received second-class honors. All graduates were bestowed with royal degrees in a ceremony held on Tuesday, January 30, 2024, at the Chiang Mai University Convention Center.
