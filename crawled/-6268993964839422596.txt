The Asia-Pacific Alliance of Health Leaders Forum (APAHL) for 2024 is set to take place from November 6 to 9, 2024. The forum's theme for this year is \"Precision Health: Enabling Personalized Care Through Digital Health Solutions\" hosted by the Faculty of Nursing Mahidol University. . The APAHL forum is an annual event that seeks to promote academic exchange programs among different institutions in the Asia-Pacific region. The forum is open to students and faculty members interested in sharing their research findings, discussing current challenges and opportunities in the healthcare industry, and exploring potential collaborations. . The event will feature keynote speeches from leading healthcare industry experts, panel discussions, and workshops covering a broad range of healthcare education, practice, and research topics. Attendees will have the opportunity to network with other participants, share their experiences, and learn from one another. Universities are participating: The University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, Yamaguchi University, Japan, International Medical University, Malaysia, Ewha Woman’s University, South Korea, Mahidol University and Chiang Mai University,Thailand.
