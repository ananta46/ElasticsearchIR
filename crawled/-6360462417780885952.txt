Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News Announcement of results of project or activity consideration supported by the 1st Digital Economy and Society Development Fund Fiscal Year 2022 27 October 2022 International College of Digital Innovation The Office of the National Digital Economy and Society Commission, supported by the Digital Economy and Society Development Fund Press, releases the results of projects or activities supported by the 1st Digital Economy and Society Development Fund for Fiscal Year 2022 at the meeting room, Vayupak 6, meeting room, 5th floor, Centra by Centara Hotel, Government Complex, and Convention Center. There was a consideration of the project proposals or activities that requested support, totaling 41 projects from government agencies, state enterprises, and educational institutions. The key objectives are to promote the development and extension of those who receive support from the 1st Digital Economy and Society Development Fund for Fiscal Year 2022, with requests of less than 10 million baht and requests of more than 10 million baht. Through monitoring and evaluating the projects by the Digital Economy and Society Fund Management Group, there are 41 projects to create an effective digital society for people and agencies in all sectors to benefit from a sustainable digital system. In this regard, Dr. Annop Thananchana, Director of the International Research and Academic Service Center, International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, Project Leader, was supported by the 1st Digital Economy and Society Development Fund for Fiscal Year 2022 in the Digital Manpower policy framework under the project titled \"Digital Manpower Ethnic and Tribal Capital Development Program\" to promote the use of digital technology to enter the labor market through lifelong education.
