The purpose of the visit was to exchange ideas on funding management under the Fundamental Fund scheme, as CMU is a budget-recipient organization.  Administrators, lecturers, staff, and researchers from higher education institutions in the Northern region attended the event, which took place in Room D206 of the D Building at the Northern Science Park on March 5, 2024.
