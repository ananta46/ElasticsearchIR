Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News ENG CMU Teams Up with Schaeffler Thailand for ‘Unlocking the Power of Bearing and Electrified Powertrains’ Training Program to Foster Knowledge among Students 7 February 2024 Faculty of Engineering Assistant Professor Dr. Pawarut Jongchansitto , the Associate Dean of the Faculty of Engineering, Chiang Mai University, had the honor of welcoming and delivering an opening speech for the training program titled \"Unlocking the Power of Bearing and Electrified Powertrains by Schaeffler Thailand\" The event took place on February 7, 2567, at 09:00 AM in the Conference Room 2 of Mechanical Engineering Building, Faculty of Engineering, Chiang Mai University. The training program was a collaborative academic effort between the Faculty of Engineering, Chiang Mai University and Schaeffler Manufacturing (Thailand) LTD. The training was privileged to feature speakers from Schaeffler Manufacturing (Thailand) Limited, including Mrs.Pachamon Panoyosuk (Head of Human Resources Thailand), Miss Varakorn Jarungsorasak (Project Managerof BD E mobility & BD Chassis system), Mr.Marvin Sumethasorn (Application Engineer) and Ms.Weeraya Sakolchai (Communication and Branding Thailand) The lecture topics covered areas such as \"Introduction to Schaeffler E-Mobility and Chassis solutions for x EV powertrain\" and \"Basic Bearing Knowledge\" These sessions aimed to provide knowledge to participating students that could be applied in their studies and future careers.
