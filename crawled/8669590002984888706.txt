This award-winning project was titled ‘The Circular Economical Production Process of High-Quality Porous Metal-organic Framework Materials of Aluminium’, which received two major awards: \"The Best Foreign Invention\" Special Award and a Gold Medal at the 17th International Warsaw Invention Show (IWIS 2023) held between December 12 – 14, 2023, at the Warsaw University of Technology, Poland.
