Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News The International College of Digital Innovation, CMU, welcomed representatives from Yidi Education and over 12 partner NGOs from the People's Republic of China for an exchange of knowledge on educational policies and the educational context in Thailand. 26 September 2024 International College of Digital Innovation On September 25, 2024, at the International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation, warmly welcomed representatives from Yidi Education and more than 12 partner NGOs from various regions of China, including Qinghai, Jiangxi, Sichuan, Tianjin, Guangdong, Henan, Shanghai, Beijing, Gansu, Shaanxi, Guangxi, Guangzhou, Yunnan, Lhasa, and the city of Alxa in Inner Mongolia. The purpose of the visit was to exchange knowledge on Thailand’s educational policies and management context, as well as to introduce Chiang Mai University's international programs to Chinese students.
