The event aimed to celebrate the Christmas festival while providing students with opportunities to engage in language and cultural exchanges. The festive gathering included various activities, such as a Best Costume competition, gift exchange activities, and a shared meal. A total of 92 students, teachers, and personnel actively participated in the festivities, which took place at the multi-purpose area of the International College of Digital Innovation at Chiang Mai University.
