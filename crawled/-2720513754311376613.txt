This evaluation aimed to gauge the development level, validate departmental operating results, identify potentials and areas for improvement, and seek guidance for advancing organizational excellence. The committee, chaired by Asst. Prof. Dr. Sitthicha Siriaree from the Faculty of Medicine, along with members Assoc. Prof. Dr. Hathaikan Chowwanapoonpohn, Asst. Prof. Dr. Sasitorn Baipong, and Asst. Prof. Pisit Sreeprasert, honored the institution. The event unfolded in three stages: 1. The initial segment featured a presentation of operational results by the College's Senior Leader team, led by Dean Asst. Prof. Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen. 2. The subsequent phase involved the executive team, comprised of Senior Leaders, participating in interviews to exchange insights on fostering an entrepreneurial school. 3. The concluding session saw the ICDI's management and personnel receiving feedback on the assessment results. This information will guide the formulation of an organizational development plan (Improvement Plan) based on the committee's observations.
