The program was from April, 21-30, 2022 (10 days period). Chiang Mai University has academic cooperation with the University of ?vora for the exchange of academic individuals in the science field to visit the University of ?vora for 10 days to exchange academic expertise. Dr. Cattaliya’s main objectives of participating in the project were: 1. to exchange research experiences between both universities; 2. to develop my teaching experiences (in the areas of Quality Management, Risk Management, Nursing Informatics, and Laws & Ethics Related to the Nursing Profession) with the Hybrid learning methodologies; and 3. to share my collaborative teaching experiences. Dr. Cattaliya was warmly welcomed by the School of Nursing at the University of ?vora as a result of her participation in the program, and they are interested in working more with the Faculty of Nursing in the future. For example, in the area of exchanging professors between the two institutions via digital systems, which includes the sharing of research data and related materials.
