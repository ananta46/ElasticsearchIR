Prof. Apichoke Lekagul, Vice Dean, together with the administrators and heads of department of the Faculty of Architecture, Chiang Mai University, were welcomed by Prof. Chien-Hsu Chen, Dean, and Prof. Yi-Hsuan Tu, Chair of the Department of Architecture. They were given an overview and visited the Working Space RAC-Coon, a research and innovation area in architecture that develops an automatic robot system for construction in universities and Taiwan on March 1, 2023. This event was conducted as part of a fieldtrip to enhance the management efficiency of the administrators of the Faculty of Architecture, Chiang Mai University. The purpose is to apply successful ideas and case studies from educational institutions abroad in formulating policies, strategies, and plans to elevate the unit to an international level. Furthermore, it aims to develop working concepts, enhance the work experience of administrators and staff, and establish academic cooperation networks with foreign countries.
