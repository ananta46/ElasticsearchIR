Pin Malakul Room, Office of the University 1, Professor Dr. Pongruk Sribanditmongkol, CMU President, accepted a donation of 220,000 baht from Mr. Bovornweth Tantranon, Co-Managing Director for Operation of Choice Ministore Co, Ltd, to support the material costs in building dust-free rooms in child development centers in the Northern region.  The project aims to increase safe spaces for youths, who are vulnerable to the impacts of PM 2.5, as well as provide environmental support for safe learning and growth.  Also, in attendance to receive the donation was Associate Professor Dr. Sirichai Koonaphapdeelert, Director of CMU Energy Research and Development Institute of Nakornping (ERDI).
