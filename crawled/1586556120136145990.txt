The event was honored by Mr. Chumnat Maneesiri, General Manager at the Integrated Biomass Management Center, CMU, as a speaker on the knowledge of waste separation before disposal properly, cultivating a conscience to save the world by conserving the environment, taking part in supporting the efficient management of solid waste, and applying the knowledge and experience gained to daily life. Additionally, the event also provided opportunities for study visits to Biogas alternative energy production from solid waste for waste management, along with the production and utilization of biogas as renewable energy and waste management as renewable fuel at Chiang Mai University. The event was held on Tuesday, September 13, 2022, from 9:00 a.m. –12:00 p.m. A total of 20 ICDI staff participated in this event.
