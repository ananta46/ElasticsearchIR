National Chung Hsing University (NCHU), Taiwan to visit the Northern Regional Veterinary Healthcare and Learning Center (Novel CMU) and the Veterinary Anatomy and Pathology Museum on 24 January 2025. 25 January 2025 Faculty of Veterinary Medicine Professor Dr. Korakot Nganvongpanit, Dean of the Faculty of Veterinary Medicine at Chiang Mai University welcomed a delegation from National Chung Hsing University (NCHU), Taiwan including Prof. Dr. Fuh-Jyh Jan, President of National Chung Hsing University (NCHU), along with Dr. Chi-Chung Chou, Senior Vice President for International Affairs, Dr. Ter-Hsin Dean, Dean of the College of Veterinary Medicine, and Dr. Chompunut Lumsangkul, Assistant Professor to visit the Northern Regional Veterinary Healthcare and Learning Center (Novel CMU) and the Veterinary Anatomy and Pathology Museum on 24 January 2025. . During this visit, both institutions discussed opportunities for academic and research collaborations and exchanged views on future partnerships.
