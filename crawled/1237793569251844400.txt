Asst. Prof. Dr. Khongphu Nimanandh, dean of CMUBS, and Dr. Pimtong Tavitiyaman, associate division head and principal lecture of CPCE, represented the faculties, with Dr. Rawi Roongruangsee, assistant dean, and Asst.
