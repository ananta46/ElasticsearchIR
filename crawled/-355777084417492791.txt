The training sessions are led by expert faculty members from Sasin School of Management of Chulalongkorn University. 10 June 2024 International College of Digital Innovation International College of Digital Innovation organized workshop training sessions to reskills/upskills/new skills all academic staffs under the ICDI Faculty Development program 2024. The training sessions are led by expert faculty members from Sasin School of Management of Chulalongkorn University. This training took place on May 27-28, 2024 at Kantary Hills Hotel Chiang Mai and June 4-6, 2024 at Ratilanna Riverside Spa Resort. The topics cover various aspects of business management, such as Disruption Workshop, Design Thinking Workshop, AI for Business, Finance for Non-finance, Marketing, and Strategy.   The aim of this training is to increase the knowledge of  ICDI faculty members in business operations, which the goal to support teaching of startup businesses and entrepreneurship courses to ICDI students and prepare the faculty members before the start of the new academic year. ICDI is dedicated to developing both academic and supporting staffs through regular trainings and seminars.
