Led by Assistant Professor Dr. Rujira Ouncharoen, Dean of ICDI, the event brought together administrators, faculty members, and supporting personnel from both institutions to foster collaboration in international education programs. Discussions revolved around curriculum management, teaching, and learning strategies at the master's and doctoral levels, and the promotion of impactful research and publications in prestigious international journals. This exchange of knowledge and experiences marks the beginning of a promising partnership dedicated to empowering future global leaders through transformative education and innovative research efforts. A total of 60 individuals participated in the event held at Ruen Ang Kaew Meeting Room, Chiang Mai University, symbolizing the spirit of cooperation and mutual respect between ICDI and BUUIC. Both colleges expressed their enthusiasm for this collaborative endeavor and look forward to continued cooperation that will nurture talents, push the boundaries of education, and create a lasting positive impact on society in the digital era.
