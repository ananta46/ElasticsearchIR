We were delighted to meet with representatives from National University of Singapore (Global), Singapore University of Social Sciences (SUSS), Ngee Ann Polytechnic, and Republic Polytechnic. These discussions focused on developing impactful study abroad programs for 2025, designed to enhance cross-cultural learning and foster academic excellence. Our visit also included a special courtesy call to the Royal Thai Embassy Singapore, where we had the privilege of meeting the Deputy Chief of Mission. This meaningful exchange highlighted shared goals in promoting Thai language and culture, while also exploring how education can play a vital role in regional collaboration. These engagements reflect our ongoing commitment to creating enriching international experiences for students and showcasing Thailand’s unique heritage on the global stage.
