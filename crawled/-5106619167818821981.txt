Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News ICDI-CMU conducted a session to publicize Chiang Mai University's international programs at Yunnan Hengshui Experimental Middle School, Xishan Campus in Kunming, Yunnan Province, People's Republic of China. 22 November 2023 International College of Digital Innovation On November 21, 2023, at 6:45 p.m. (People's Republic of China time), the International College of Digital Innovation at Chiang Mai University, led by Assistant Professor Dr. Benjamas Suksatit, Associate Dean, and accompanied by the faculty, conducted a session to publicize Chiang Mai University's international programs at Yunnan Hengshui Experimental Middle School, Xishan Campus in Kunming, Yunnan Province, People's Republic of China. During this event, Lecturer Dr. Worawit Tepsan and Lecturer Dr. Aniwat Phaphuangwittayakul introduced Chiang Mai University and promoted its international programs. All 17 bachelor's degree programs were presented to over 200 high school students interested in studying at Chiang Mai University and in Thailand.
