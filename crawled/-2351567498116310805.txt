KAIST University was the host of the event taking place between December 4 – 7, 2023, in Seoul, South Korea. The winning project was titled ‘Designing Persuasive User Interface by User Personality to Enhance Engagement for Survey Application’, by Ms. Patcharaphon Sribunthankul, a first-year Master’s student in Software Engineering. Her supervisor was Assistant Professor Dr. Noppon Choosri from the same programme. The first runner-up was ‘A Comparison of Prediction Digital Major in Undergraduate with Learning Styles Model’ by Ms. Ratchathida Phummaphut, a first-year Master’s student in Digital Technology Management. She was supervised by Dr. Jirapipat Thanyaphongphat and Dr. Wantana Areeprayolkij. The second runner-up was ‘Creating Game-based Social-Emotional Learning Tools in Montessori Schools Environment for Children’s House Students’ by Ms. Olivia Bova, a first-year PhD student in Knowledge and Innovation Management. She was supervised by Assistant Professor Dr. Noppon Choosri. ?
