The event aimed to warmly welcome new undergraduate and graduate students. It was honored by the presence of Assistant Professor Dr. Benjamas Suksatit, Vice Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation, as well as faculty members, staff, and students from both undergraduate and graduate programs.
