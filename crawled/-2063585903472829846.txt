ICDI organized a workshop and learning exchange seminar for all supporting staff on the topic of \"Using Electronic Document System at Chiang Mai University (CMU e-Document)\" on February 6, 2023. The event was honored by Ms. Wanna Kerdlarp, Head of General Administration, and Ms. Sayanil Pracharat, from the Faculty of Mass Communication, who introduced and reviewed the process of using the system. This activity is one of the strategies to drive the organization towards digital transformation, with the aim to reduce the use of paper and become more efficient and faster in communication and coordination. This activity was divided into 4 sessions; 2 sessions for supporting staff and another 2 sessions for academic staff.
