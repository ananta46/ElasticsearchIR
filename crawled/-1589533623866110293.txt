The symposium, held from August 27-29, 2024, at Kantary Hills Hotel in Chiang Mai, aims to foster academic collaboration and enhance research networks between Chiang Mai University, Kagawa University, and National Chiayi University. The selected students are: 1.\tMs. Kornkamon Liebsuetragul 2.\tMs. Khao Marukapitak 3.\tMs. Nutchaya Worapanyasakulchai 4.\tMs. Witchawee Noinivorn 5.\tMr. Chanchai Chan These students were chosen based on the quality and relevance of their research to the symposium's themes of climate action and sustainability. Their participation not only highlights the caliber of education and research at ICDI but also contributes to the global dialogue on addressing social development goal (SDGs). This symposium underscores the commitment of the three universities to advancing sustainable development through international collaboration, providing students and faculty alike with opportunities to engage in meaningful research and dialogue on global issues.
