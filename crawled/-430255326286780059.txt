The purpose of the event was to provide students with knowledge and understanding of Chinese culture and the significance of the Mid-Autumn Festival, a major celebration in both China and Chinese communities worldwide. The festival typically takes place in September each year and is known as a celebration of the autumn harvest. In Chinese, the Mid-Autumn Festival is often referred to as the \"Festival of the Mid-Autumn Season.\" It is celebrated by eating mooncakes and sharing meals with family members, making it an important occasion for family reunions.
