Powerpoint Template Q&A Privacy Policy Contact Suggestion News CDI-CMU being a host for the 7th Thailand International College Consortium International Conference 2023 8 February 2023 International College of Digital Innovation The International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, in collaboration with the International College Network of Thailand, whose members consist of: (1) Silpakorn University International College (2) International College for Sustainable Studies, Srinakharinwirot University (3) Naresuan University International College (4) Khon Kaen University International College 5) Burapha University International College (6) Prince of Songkla University Hat Yai Campus International College (7) Faculty of International Studies, Prince of Songkhla University, Phuket Campus Professor of Practice Dr. Charin Techapun, Vice President of Chiang Mai University, was honored to attend and gave an opening speech. Dr. Gunter Faltin of the Entrepreneurship Department, Free University of Berlin, Germany, and Dr. Vincente Ramos Mir of the Department of Applied Economics, University of the Balearic Islands, Spain, were keynote speakers for the lectures on the topics of \"Economic Applications of Emerging Geospatial Data for Managing Smart Tourism Destinations\" and \"Economic Applications of Emerging Geospatial Data for Managing Sustainable Tourism Destinations,\" respectively. The event drew more than 65 academic papers from 11 institutions around the world, and attendees included academics, researchers, lecturers in educational institutions, and students and graduate students from more than 120 departments, as well as interested people from both domestic and international locations.
