Chuseok, or Mid-Autumn Festival, is one of the most important and festive holidays of the year. The festival is held on the 15th of the 8th lunar month. The activity provides opportunities for CMU’s students to cross-culturally learn in order to appreciate cultural diversity, especially in Korean culture.
