The Student Quality Development Unit and the Student Union of the International College of Digital Innovation, Chiang Mai University, organized the Chiang Mai University International Day \"Inter Cup 2023\" under the Study Explore Embrace Diffuse Program (SEED Program). The event aimed to promote CMU Smart Health and to strengthen the good relationship among CMU international students. The event had a total of 6 teams competing in the football tournament, consisting of international students from various faculties, including the Faculty of Business Administration, Faculty of Nursing, Faculty of Science, Faculty of Economics, Faculty of Social Sciences, College of Arts Media and Technology, and International College of Digital Innovation. The event exuded an atmosphere of excitement, and the athletes displayed admirable sportsmanship. The event was held at All Star Arena Chiang Mai from 12:00 PM to 06:00 PM on Saturday, February 25, 2022, with a total number of 72 international CMU students and staff participating.
