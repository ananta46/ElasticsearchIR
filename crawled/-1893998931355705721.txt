Hosted by the International College of Digital Innovation, the 76th Independence Day of Pakistan 2023 was commemorated with the students of Chiang Mai University. The event's primary objective was to provide Chiang Mai University students with a unique opportunity to explore and learn about the rich cultural heritage of Pakistan. This event was held on Monday, August 14, 2023, at the International College of Digital Innovation. The occasion saw enthusiastic participation from 60 international students, faculty members, and staff, all collectively engaged in celebrating and fostering cultural diversity.
