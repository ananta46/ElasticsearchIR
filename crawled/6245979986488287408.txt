As part of their visit, the students had the opportunity to explore both regular and international programs of CMU, while also learning about university life and extracurricular. During their time at CMU, the students engaged in a fruitful exchange of academic knowledge. This visit aims to foster cross-cultural understanding and promote the intellectual growth of Chiang Mai University, bridging the gap between educational institutions from different regions. This event was held on Wednesday, July 5, 2023, Associate Professor Dr. Manad Khamkong, Associate Dean of the International College of Digital Innovation, delivered a heartfelt welcome speech, accompanied by the directors of Tzu Chi University Demonstration High School, esteemed teachers, and 32 enthusiastic students.
